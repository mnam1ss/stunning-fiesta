A BBC investigation has uncovered that criminal gangs in London are systematically recruiting teenage girls and coercing them into sexual exploitation. The report indicates these operations are widespread across the capital and involve perpetrators from diverse ethnic backgrounds.

The investigation details how the gangs use various methods to target and groom vulnerable girls and young women, often establishing control before forcing them into sexual situations. The findings highlight a significant and organized pattern of abuse affecting numerous victims.

**Key points:**
*   An investigation has revealed organized criminal gangs in London are exploiting teenage girls.
*   The perpetrators come from a range of ethnic backgrounds and operate across the city.
*   The gangs use grooming tactics to coerce young women into forced sexual activity.
