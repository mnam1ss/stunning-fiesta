Newly released images from court documents related to the late financier Jeffrey Epstein have surfaced, reportedly depicting Prince Andrew in a casual setting. The photographs, which have circulated online, appear to show the Duke of York on the floor with a woman present.

These images emerge from a trove of judicial records and add to the longstanding public scrutiny regarding the prince's association with Epstein. Prince Andrew has consistently denied any misconduct in relation to his past friendship with the convicted sex offender.

The release of the pictures has prompted renewed media attention and public discussion about the royal's connections, though their specific context and provenance remain subjects of reporting by various news outlets.
