**Visual Evidence Emerges of Airstrikes in Iran**

Newly verified video footage from multiple locations within Iran shows significant explosions occurring overnight. The material, authenticated by international broadcasters and news agencies, depicts large detonations illuminating the sky in several areas. Reports from regional sources attribute the strikes to military actions by the United States and Israel, though official statements from all involved governments are pending. The circulating videos provide the first widespread visual confirmation of the scale of the reported operations, which appear to have targeted multiple sites. The situation remains fluid, with details on specific locations, intended targets, and potential casualties not yet independently confirmed. The development marks a notable escalation in regional tensions.

Key points:
*   Verified video evidence shows substantial explosions at multiple sites across Iran.
*   The strikes are widely reported to be operations conducted by US and Israeli forces.
*   The extent of damage and any casualties remains unconfirmed by independent sources.
