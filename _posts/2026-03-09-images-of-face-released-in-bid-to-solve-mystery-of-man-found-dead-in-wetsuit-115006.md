Police in the United Kingdom have released facial reconstruction images in an effort to identify a man found deceased over a year ago. The individual was discovered on a beach in Kent in December 2022, wearing a wetsuit. Despite extensive inquiries, his identity has remained unknown.

Investigators from the Kent and Essex Serious Crime Directorate commissioned the new images, which depict how the man may have looked in life. Authorities are appealing to the public for any information that could help solve the case, which has been open for 18 months. The man is described as being of slim build, approximately 5 feet 9 inches tall, and in his 40s or 50s.

Key points:
*   Facial reconstruction images have been released to identify a man found dead on a Kent beach in December 2022.
*   The unidentified man was wearing a wetsuit when discovered.
*   Police inquiries have so far been unsuccessful over an 18-month period.
