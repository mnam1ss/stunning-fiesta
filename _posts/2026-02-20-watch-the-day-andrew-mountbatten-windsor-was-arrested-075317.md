**Former Prince Andrew Spends Day at Police Station in Norfolk**

Video footage has been released showing the arrival of Andrew Mountbatten-Windsor, the Duke of York, at a police station in Norfolk. The former prince was present at the station for the duration of the day before being allowed to leave. Authorities confirmed he was released under investigation, a standard procedure where an individual is not formally charged but remains part of an ongoing inquiry. The visit was reportedly in connection with a pre-arranged appointment. No further details regarding the nature of the investigation were provided by officials at this time.

Key points:
*   Andrew Mountbatten-Windsor attended a police station in Norfolk for a full day.
*   He was released under investigation, meaning no charges have been filed.
*   The visit was a pre-arranged appointment related to an ongoing police inquiry.
