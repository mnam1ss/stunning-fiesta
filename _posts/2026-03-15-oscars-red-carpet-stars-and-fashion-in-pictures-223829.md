Hollywood celebrities gathered for the 96th Academy Awards ceremony on Sunday, arriving at the Dolby Theatre in Los Angeles. The event, which honors cinematic achievements, began with the traditional procession along the red carpet, where attendees showcased their formal attire.

Photographers documented the arrivals as actors, directors, and other industry figures paused for pictures. The fashion choices included a range of designers, with many outfits featuring elaborate detailing and classic silhouettes. The arrivals portion of the evening precedes the main ceremony, where awards in categories such as Best Picture and acting are presented.

Key points:
*   The Oscars ceremony commenced with celebrity arrivals on the red carpet in Los Angeles.
*   Attendees displayed a variety of formal wear from numerous fashion designers.
*   The red carpet event is a prelude to the main awards presentation inside the theater.
