The 2024 BAFTA Film Awards ceremony took place in London on Sunday, featuring a gathering of prominent film industry figures and notable guests. The event was marked by several notable moments, including a public appearance by actors Jeremy Allen White and Ayo Edebiri, who arrived together on the red carpet. During the ceremony, host David Tennant offered an apology for the use of strong language in a previous broadcast segment. The afternoon also saw an appearance by a performer in a Paddington Bear costume, adding a lighthearted element to the proceedings. Unseasonably clear and sunny weather was noted by attendees at the outdoor portions of the event.

Key points:
- Actors Jeremy Allen White and Ayo Edebiri made a joint red carpet appearance.
- Host David Tennant apologized for prior use of strong language on air.
- The event included sunny weather and an appearance by Paddington Bear.
