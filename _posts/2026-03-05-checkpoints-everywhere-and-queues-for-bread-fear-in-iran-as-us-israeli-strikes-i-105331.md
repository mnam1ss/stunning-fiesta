Residents across Iran are reporting heightened security measures and disruptions to daily life as military strikes attributed to U.S. and Israeli forces continue. Widespread internet outages have become frequent, complicating communication for families attempting to maintain contact. The internal security presence has increased significantly, with numerous checkpoints established in urban areas.

Basic necessities are also affected, with citizens facing long lines at bakeries and other essential supply points. The ongoing situation has created an atmosphere of apprehension as the aerial campaign persists without a clear resolution in sight. These conditions compound the challenges for the civilian population managing routine affairs amid the instability.

Key points:
• Increased military strikes have led to a major expansion of security checkpoints within Iranian cities.
• Frequent internet blackouts are severely hindering personal communications and information flow.
• Civilian life is disrupted, marked by shortages of essentials like bread, resulting in lengthy queues.
