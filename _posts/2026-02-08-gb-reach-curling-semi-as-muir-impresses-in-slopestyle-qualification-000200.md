Team Great Britain's mixed doubles curling pair has secured a spot in the semi-finals of the Winter Olympic Games. The team advanced with two preliminary round matches remaining, following consecutive wins against strong opponents from Canada and the United States. These results preserved the British duo's undefeated record in the tournament so far.

In other events, British athlete Kirsty Muir delivered a notable performance during the women's freestyle skiing slopestyle qualification round. Her score placed her in a strong position to advance to the final, contributing to a positive day for the British team across multiple sports.

Key points:
- Great Britain's mixed doubles curling team qualified for the semi-finals early.
- The curling pair remains undefeated after victories over Canada and the U.S.
- Skier Kirsty Muir performed well in her slopestyle qualification round.
