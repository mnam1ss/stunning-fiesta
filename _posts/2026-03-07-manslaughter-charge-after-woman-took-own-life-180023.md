A man has been charged in connection with the death of a woman who took her own life. Seyhan Assaf faces allegations of manslaughter and controlling or coercive behaviour following the passing of Gillian Morand. The charges were brought by authorities after an investigation into the circumstances surrounding Morand's death. Legal proceedings are now underway, with the case moving through the criminal justice system. The development marks a significant step in a case that links the alleged conduct of one individual to the suicide of another. Further details regarding the specific allegations have not been disclosed publicly at this stage.

Key points:
*   Seyhan Assaf is charged with manslaughter and coercive control.
*   The charges relate to the death of Gillian Morand.
*   The case is now subject to ongoing legal proceedings.
