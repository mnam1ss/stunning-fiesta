Police have opened a murder investigation following the death of an infant. The inquiry was initiated after emergency services responded to a call at a residential address. A 43-year-old woman was apprehended by officers at the location and has been formally arrested on suspicion of murder. Authorities have confirmed she is currently being held in police custody for questioning. The identity of the child and the exact circumstances leading to the death have not been publicly disclosed, as the investigation is in its early stages. Detectives are conducting a thorough examination of the scene and have appealed for the public to avoid speculation. Further updates from law enforcement are expected as the inquiry progresses.

Key points:
- A murder investigation is underway following the death of a baby.
- A 43-year-old woman was arrested at the scene and remains in custody.
- Police are continuing their investigation and have asked the public not to speculate.
