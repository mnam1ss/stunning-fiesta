**Former Team GB Bobsleigher to Compete for Jamaica in Upcoming Winter Games**

Joel Fearon, a bobsleigh athlete who previously won a medal with Great Britain at the 2014 Winter Olympics, is preparing for his third appearance at the Games. Fearon had retired from the sport but has since returned to competition, this time representing Jamaica. His journey to elite winter sports included working as a pizza delivery driver earlier in his career. The athlete is now training for the upcoming Winter Olympics as part of the Jamaican bobsleigh team.

Key points:
*   Joel Fearon is a bobsleigh athlete who earned an Olympic medal with Team GB in 2014.
*   After a period of retirement, he has returned to the sport and will now compete for Jamaica.
*   He is currently training for his third Winter Olympic appearance.
