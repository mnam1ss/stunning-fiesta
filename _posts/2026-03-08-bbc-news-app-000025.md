The BBC has announced an update to its official mobile news application, designed to provide users with a more personalized and comprehensive news experience. The app aggregates content from across the BBC's global news operation, offering a central hub for readers. It is structured to deliver major headlines and developing stories, alongside the broadcaster's live coverage of events as they happen. A central feature allows individuals to customize their feed by selecting specific subjects and regions they wish to follow more closely. The application is available for free download on major smartphone platforms, with content accessible in the UK and internationally. The update reflects a continued shift toward digital and on-demand news consumption.

Key points:
*   The updated app provides access to major headlines, live reporting, and ongoing news coverage.
*   Users can tailor their news feed by choosing to follow specific topics and geographical areas.
*   The application is free to download and is available for use both within the United Kingdom and overseas.
