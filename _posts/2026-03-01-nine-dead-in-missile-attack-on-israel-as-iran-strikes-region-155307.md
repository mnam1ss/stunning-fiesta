A series of missile and drone attacks launched by Iran against Israel has resulted in at least nine fatalities, according to initial reports. The strikes, which occurred overnight, mark a significant escalation in regional hostilities. Iranian state media stated the operation was a direct response to what it called recent aggressive actions by Israel.

The attack involved numerous projectiles, with Israeli defenses intercepting a large number. Damage was reported at a military base in southern Israel. Broader regional tensions have been high following a recent strike on an Iranian diplomatic compound in Syria, which Iran blamed on Israel.

**Key points:**
*   Iranian forces launched a direct missile and drone attack on Israeli territory.
*   Israeli officials report at least nine deaths and damage to a military facility.
*   Iran describes the operation as retaliation for perceived Israeli actions.
