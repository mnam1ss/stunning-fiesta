India has successfully defended its T20 World Cup championship, securing a decisive victory over New Zealand in the final match held in Ahmedabad. The win comes one year after a significant tournament loss, marking a strong return to form for the team.

The Indian side posted a substantial total while batting first. In response, the New Zealand team was unable to mount a successful chase, falling short by a significant margin of 96 runs. The comprehensive nature of the victory underscored India's dominant performance throughout the contest.

This result confirms India's continued status as a leading force in international T20 cricket, adding another title to its record.

Key points:
* India defeated New Zealand by 96 runs to win the T20 World Cup.
* The final was played in Ahmedabad, with India defending its title from the previous tournament.
* The victory follows India's loss in a major international final the previous year.
