**UK Experiences Dramatic Weather Shifts Within Two Days**

The United Kingdom has witnessed a series of starkly contrasting weather events over a 48-hour period. The conditions included a notable influx of Saharan dust, the hottest day recorded so far this year, and snowfall in some regions.

This follows a recent spell of relatively mild and dry spring weather. Forecasters indicate a significant shift is expected in the coming week, with conditions predicted to become colder, wetter, and windier across the country. The rapid succession of different weather types highlights the dynamic and changeable nature of the UK's climate during seasonal transitions.

Key points:
*   The UK saw Saharan dust, record heat for 2024, and snow all within two days.
*   The weather is forecast to turn colder, wetter, and more windy next week.
*   The events mark a sharp change from recent mild and dry spring conditions.
