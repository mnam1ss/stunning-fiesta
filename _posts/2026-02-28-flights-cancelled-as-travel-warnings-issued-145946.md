Several major airlines have announced significant schedule changes following recent security incidents, leading to widespread flight cancellations. British Airways, Virgin Atlantic, and Wizz Air are among the carriers adjusting their operations in response to the events. Travel advisories have been issued for affected regions, prompting airlines to reroute or suspend service to ensure passenger safety. The operational overhaul is causing considerable disruption for travelers, with airlines advising customers to check the status of their flights directly. Airport authorities are working with carriers to manage the logistical impact and assist stranded passengers. Further updates on schedule revisions are expected from the airlines in the coming days.

Key points:
- Multiple airlines, including British Airways and Virgin Atlantic, are altering flight schedules.
- The changes are a direct response to recent security incidents and official travel warnings.
- Passengers are experiencing cancellations and are urged to confirm their travel plans with their airline.
