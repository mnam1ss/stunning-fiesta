A 15-year-old boy has been taken into custody following a stabbing incident at a school in England. The event occurred on Monday morning, prompting an immediate emergency response. According to reports, students and staff were instructed to take shelter in classrooms as the situation developed.

Police and ambulance services were dispatched to the scene. A teenage girl was treated for a stab wound and transported to a hospital; her injuries are not believed to be life-threatening. The suspect was arrested at the location on suspicion of attempted murder. The school has confirmed the incident and stated it is cooperating fully with the ongoing police investigation. Officials have assured parents that there is no further threat to the school community.

Key points:
• A 15-year-old boy was arrested after a teenage girl was stabbed at a school.
• The injured girl was hospitalized with non-life-threatening injuries.
• The school is cooperating with police, who say there is no continuing threat.
