Police in Tumbler Ridge have named an 18-year-old as the primary suspect following a series of violent incidents that resulted in multiple fatalities. The events unfolded at a local school, where authorities report six individuals were killed and more than two dozen were wounded. In a related development, two additional deceased persons were discovered at a residence in the vicinity. The suspect's identity has not been publicly released, and the motive for the attacks remains under active investigation. Law enforcement officials have stated the immediate threat to the community has been contained, though the investigation is ongoing. The community has been urged to avoid the affected areas as police continue their work.

Key points:
• An 18-year-old has been identified as a suspect in the Tumbler Ridge incidents.
• The violence resulted in eight total fatalities: six at a school and two at a nearby home, with over 25 people injured.
• The investigation is active, with the motive unknown and the immediate threat reported as contained.
