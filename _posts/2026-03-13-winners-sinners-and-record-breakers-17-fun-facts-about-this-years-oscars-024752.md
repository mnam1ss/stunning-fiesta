The 96th Academy Awards ceremony has concluded, recognizing achievements in film from the past year. The event featured a diverse slate of nominated works, including major studio productions and independent films. Several movies received multiple nominations across various categories such as acting, directing, and technical crafts.

The ceremony followed the traditional format of award presentations interspersed with performances and commemorative segments. As is customary, the awards for Best Picture, Directing, and the four acting categories were among the final presentations of the evening. The event also included the annual "In Memoriam" tribute to industry members who died in the past year.

Key points:
*   The ceremony honored films from a broad range of genres and production scales.
*   Awards were distributed across categories recognizing artistic and technical filmmaking.
*   The event maintained its standard structure of awards, performances, and tributes.
