Residents of Tehran have reported experiencing what they described as the most severe night of Israeli airstrikes to date, according to accounts gathered by the BBC. Witnesses in the Iranian capital spoke of ongoing and powerful detonations throughout the night. The reported escalation follows a public statement from the United States indicating that military operations in the region are expected to increase in intensity.

The situation contributes to heightened regional tensions, with international observers closely monitoring developments. Official statements from the involved governments regarding the specific details of the overnight events are still forthcoming. The reports from Tehran underscore a significant increase in military activity.

Key points:
• Tehran residents reported an intense night of airstrikes attributed to Israel.
• The United States had previously warned that military attacks would intensify.
• The events mark a notable escalation in ongoing regional hostilities.
