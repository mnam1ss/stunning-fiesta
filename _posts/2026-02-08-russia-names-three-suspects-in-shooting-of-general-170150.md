Russian authorities have identified three individuals as suspects in the shooting of a high-ranking military officer. According to official statements, the primary suspect is the alleged gunman, who is accused of fleeing to the United Arab Emirates following the incident. The other two named individuals are believed to have been involved in the attack's planning or execution.

The investigation into the assault on the general is ongoing, with Russian officials coordinating with international partners regarding the suspects. No further details about the current whereabouts of the accused or the specific charges have been released at this time. The case has drawn significant attention due to the seniority of the targeted officer.

Key points:
- Russian officials have named three suspects in the shooting of a general.
- The alleged gunman is reported to have fled to the UAE after the attack.
- The investigation remains active, with details on charges and extradition pending.
