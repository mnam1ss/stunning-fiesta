A driver has been sentenced to prison after a fatal collision in which he mistakenly pressed the accelerator instead of the brake. The incident resulted in the death of 20-year-old university student Aalia Mahomed.

According to court reports, the driver lost control of his vehicle, which mounted the pavement and struck Ms. Mahomed. She was pronounced dead at the scene. During the sentencing hearing, a family statement described the profound loss felt by her loved ones.

The judge stated that the driver's actions, though not deliberate, constituted a serious lapse in the standard of care expected on the road, leading to a tragic and avoidable outcome. The driver was convicted of causing death by dangerous driving.

Key points:
• A driver was jailed for causing death by dangerous driving after confusing the accelerator and brake.
• The collision killed 20-year-old student Aalia Mahomed.
• The court emphasized the incident was a fatal error with serious consequences.
