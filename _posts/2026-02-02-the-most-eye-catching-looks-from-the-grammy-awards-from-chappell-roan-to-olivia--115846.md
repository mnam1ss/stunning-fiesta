The 66th Grammy Awards ceremony in Los Angeles on Sunday featured a wide array of fashion statements from attending musicians and performers. Attendees showcased styles ranging from bold, theatrical ensembles to classic, tailored suits on the red carpet.

Several artists received particular attention for their sartorial choices. Emerging and established performers alike selected outfits that reflected both personal style and the celebratory nature of the event. The looks presented a mix of high-fashion designs and custom creations, contributing to the visual spectacle of the music industry's annual gathering.

Key points:
*   Numerous musicians made notable fashion statements on the red carpet at the Los Angeles event.
*   Styles displayed included both dramatic, eye-catching designs and elegant, traditional formalwear.
*   The variety of outfits highlighted individual artists' styles and the festive atmosphere of the awards ceremony.
