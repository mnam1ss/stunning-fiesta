**Israeli Public Shows Support for Military Action Against Iran**

A recent survey indicates that a majority of the Israeli public continues to endorse the ongoing joint military campaign with the United States targeting Iranian interests. This support persists despite a widespread sense of weariness among the population due to frequent security disruptions.

The findings suggest that strategic concerns regarding Iran's regional activities and nuclear program remain a dominant factor in public opinion. Citizens report experiencing fatigue from repeated air raid sirens and the general instability associated with prolonged conflict. Analysts note that this sustained backing provides the Israeli government with significant domestic latitude to continue its current security policies, even as the long-term trajectory of the campaign remains unclear.

Key points:
*   Polling shows majority Israeli support for the US-Israeli military campaign against Iran.
*   This support coexists with public fatigue over constant security alerts and upheaval.
*   The sustained public backing is seen as bolstering the government's strategic position.
