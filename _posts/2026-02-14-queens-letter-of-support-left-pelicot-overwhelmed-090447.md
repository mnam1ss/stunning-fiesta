Queen Sends Private Letter to Advocate Following Public Testimony

Buckingham Palace has confirmed that Queen Camilla has written a private letter to Gisèle Pelicot, a survivor and advocate. The correspondence was sent in response to Ms. Pelicot's recent public testimony about her personal experiences. In the message, the Queen acknowledged the impact of Pelicot's actions, stating they had established a significant and enduring influence on public attitudes.

Ms. Pelicot, who received the letter, described feeling deeply moved by the gesture. A palace spokesperson emphasized the Queen's longstanding commitment to supporting individuals on related issues. The private note is seen as a symbolic gesture of recognition from the monarchy toward advocacy work in this area.

Key points:
• Queen Camilla sent a private letter to advocate Gisèle Pelicot.
• The letter recognized the lasting impact of Pelicot's public testimony.
• The gesture aligns with the Queen's recognized support for related causes.
