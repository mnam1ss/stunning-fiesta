Smoke was seen rising over Tehran early Friday following reports of explosions heard in several parts of Iran. The incident occurred amid heightened regional tensions. The BBC has verified footage showing plumes of smoke in the Iranian capital, though the exact cause and locations of the reported blasts have not been officially confirmed by Iranian authorities.

Separately, former U.S. President Donald Trump commented on the situation, stating that what he described as "major combat operations" had commenced. This statement was reported from Jerusalem. There has been no independent verification linking the events in Iran directly to any specific military action. Iranian state media has not provided immediate details on the source of the explosions or any potential casualties.

Key points:
• Explosions were reported and smoke was seen in Tehran, with the cause unconfirmed by officials.
• Former U.S. President Donald Trump stated that "major combat operations" had begun.
• Iranian state media had not released immediate details on the incidents or casualties.
