Annabel Schofield, a model and actress known for her role on the television series *Dallas*, has died at the age of 62. Her passing was confirmed by representatives, though no cause was immediately provided.

Schofield first gained prominence in the fashion industry during the 1980s. Following her modeling career, she relocated to Los Angeles to pursue acting. She secured a part on the popular prime-time soap opera *Dallas*, which aired from 1978 to 1991, appearing during its later seasons. Her work on the show remains a noted part of her professional legacy.
