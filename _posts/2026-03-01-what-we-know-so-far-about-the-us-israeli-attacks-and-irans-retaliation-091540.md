Iran has launched a series of missile and drone strikes toward Israel and several Gulf Arab states, according to international reports. The action is described as a direct retaliation for recent military actions attributed to the United States and Israel. The targeted Gulf nations are reported to host American military installations.

The attack marks a significant escalation in regional tensions. Israeli defense systems were activated in response to the incoming projectiles. There have been no immediate reports of major damage or casualties from the strikes.

Key points:
*   Iran conducted missile and drone strikes targeting Israel and multiple Gulf states.
*   The operation is stated as retaliation for prior US and Israeli military actions.
*   The situation represents a major increase in hostilities in the Middle East.
