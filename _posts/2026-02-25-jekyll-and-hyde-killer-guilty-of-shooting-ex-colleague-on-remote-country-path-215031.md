A man has been found guilty of murdering a former colleague on a remote path in Perthshire, Scotland. David Campbell, a former gamekeeper, was convicted of shooting Brian Low near Aberfeldy in February 2022.

The court heard that Campbell, described by prosecutors as having a "Jekyll and Hyde" character, killed Mr. Low and subsequently attempted to conceal evidence of the crime. The motive was reportedly linked to a prior dispute between the two men, who had previously worked together.

The investigation involved extensive forensic work in the rural area where the shooting occurred. Campbell was arrested months after the incident following a detailed police inquiry.
