**Ukrainian Military to Showcase Drone Defense Systems in Regional Exchange**

Ukrainian specialists are preparing to share their expertise on counter-drone technology with partners in the Middle East, according to recent reports. This initiative follows a visit to Ukraine by international journalists, who were shown the country's latest developments in electronic warfare and air defense systems designed to neutralize unmanned aerial vehicles. The ongoing conflict has made Ukraine a testing ground for such technologies, leading to rapid innovation.

The planned knowledge transfer indicates a growing international interest in the tactical lessons learned from the war. Observers note that several nations in the Middle East face persistent security challenges involving drone threats. The exchange is seen as part of broader diplomatic and defense cooperation efforts.

**Key points:**
*   Ukrainian experts will advise on anti-drone technology in the Middle East.
*   The systems were recently demonstrated to foreign media in Ukraine.
*   The move highlights the international demand for combat-tested drone defense strategies.
