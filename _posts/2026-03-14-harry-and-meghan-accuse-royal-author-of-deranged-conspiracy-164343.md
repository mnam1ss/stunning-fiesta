**New Royal Book Sparks Response from Duke and Duchess of Sussex**

A new biography detailing the inner workings of the British Royal Family has prompted a strong public statement from Prince Harry and Meghan, the Duchess of Sussex. The couple has criticized the author, Lady Colin Campbell, labeling her claims as a "deranged conspiracy."

The book reportedly contains assertions that the late Queen Elizabeth II privately expressed concerns, suggesting the Duchess had an undue influence over Prince Harry. These alleged remarks are central to the current dispute.

Representatives for Harry and Meghan have firmly denied the book's characterization of events. The statement aims to discredit the narrative presented, framing it as baseless speculation. The publication highlights ongoing tensions between certain authors and the Sussexes regarding the portrayal of their departure from royal duties and family relationships.

Key points:
*   The Duke and Duchess of Sussex have publicly denounced claims in a new royal biography.
*   The book alleges the late Queen made private comments about Meghan's influence on Harry.
*   The couple's representatives have categorically rejected the author's account as unfounded.
