A Scottish woman received a terminal brain cancer diagnosis following an incident where a suitcase fell on her head, according to a recent report.

Lauren Macpherson, 26, was returning from a music event in London when the accident occurred. After being struck, she sought medical attention for a headache. Scans conducted to assess the impact revealed an unrelated and aggressive brain tumor. Doctors informed her the cancer was terminal.

Macpherson has stated that while the news was devastating, the accidental discovery allowed her to begin treatment immediately. She is now focusing on raising awareness and funds for cancer research.

**Key points:**
*   A suitcase falling on a woman's head led to scans that uncovered a terminal brain tumor.
*   The 26-year-old was diagnosed after returning from a London festival.
*   The patient says the accidental discovery enabled prompt treatment initiation.
