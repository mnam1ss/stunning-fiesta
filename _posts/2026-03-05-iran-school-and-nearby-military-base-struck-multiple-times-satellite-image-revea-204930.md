Satellite imagery has confirmed multiple impacts at a school and an adjacent military facility in Iran following an attack this past Saturday. Iranian officials have reported that the incident resulted in significant casualties. The exact nature of the attack and the parties responsible have not been officially confirmed by the state.

The imagery, analyzed by independent observers, shows distinct damage to structures at both locations. The event has drawn international attention, with calls for clarity regarding the circumstances. Iranian state media has provided limited details on the operational status of the military base following the strikes. An investigation into the attack is reportedly underway.

Key points:
• Satellite images show damage to both a school and a military base in Iran from a Saturday attack.
• Iranian authorities report a high number of fatalities from the incident.
• The cause and responsible party for the strikes remain unconfirmed.
