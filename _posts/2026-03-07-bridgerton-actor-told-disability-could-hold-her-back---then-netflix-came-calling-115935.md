**Actor Reflects on Career Path Following Major Casting Role**

Gracie McGonigal, an actor who recently joined the cast of the popular period drama *Bridgerton*, has shared her perspective on navigating the industry. McGonigal stated that she was previously advised her disability could limit her acting opportunities. This changed significantly after she was cast by Netflix for the show's latest season.

Since the new season became available for streaming, McGonigal has commented on the supportive reception from the audience. She described the fan response to her involvement in the series as overwhelmingly positive.

The actor's casting is noted as part of a broader conversation about representation of disabled performers in major television and film productions.

**Key points:**
*   Gracie McGonigal received a career-changing role in *Bridgerton* after being told her disability might hinder her acting prospects.
*   The actor has reported a strongly favorable reaction from viewers since the season's release.
*   Her experience highlights ongoing discussions regarding disability representation in mainstream entertainment.
