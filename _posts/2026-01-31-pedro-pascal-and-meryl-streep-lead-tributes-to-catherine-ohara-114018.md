Canadian actress Catherine O'Hara, known for her extensive comedic work in film and television, has died at the age of 71. Her passing occurred in Los Angeles on Friday after what has been described as a short illness.

News of her death prompted public acknowledgments from numerous figures in the entertainment industry. Fellow actors Pedro Pascal and Meryl Streep were among those who shared tributes, highlighting her impact on peers and audiences alike. O'Hara's career spanned several decades, with notable roles in projects that became cultural touchstones for comedy fans.

Her work established her as a respected and influential performer in both her native Canada and internationally. Further details regarding memorial services have not been released at this time.
