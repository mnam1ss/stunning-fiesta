Parents of Girl Swept to Sea Describe Ongoing Anguish

The parents of a seven-year-old girl who was carried out to sea by a wave while on a family holiday have spoken publicly about their profound grief. Tasneem and Zubair Makda stated that the loss of their daughter has left them permanently altered, describing the emotional aftermath as a source of constant and severe distress. The incident occurred during a coastal outing. In their statement, the couple conveyed the depth of their sorrow while acknowledging the support they have received. Authorities were notified following the event, which serves as a tragic reminder of the dangers posed by unpredictable coastal conditions.

Key points:
• The parents of a seven-year-old girl have publicly addressed her death after she was swept out to sea.
• Tasneem and Zubair Makda say the tragedy has fundamentally changed them and causes enduring pain.
• The incident highlights the potential hazards of waves and strong currents during seaside visits.
