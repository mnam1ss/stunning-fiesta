Meghan, the Duchess of Sussex, will now operate her lifestyle brand, American Riviera Orchard, independently following the conclusion of a collaborative agreement with Netflix. The streaming service had provided production and promotional support for the brand's initial launch in March of last year. The partnership was part of a broader content creation deal between Netflix and the Duke and Duchess of Sussex's Archewell Productions company.

A representative for the couple confirmed the end of this specific brand partnership, noting that both parties remain mutually committed to their ongoing creative projects. The brand, which focuses on home and garden products, recently sent out its first product—a strawberry jam—to a select group of influencers. The separation allows the Duchess to manage the brand's direction without the formal involvement of the streaming platform.

Key points:
*   Meghan's American Riviera Orchard brand will no longer be supported by Netflix.
*   The collaboration helped launch the lifestyle brand in March 2023.
*   The broader content deal between Archewell Productions and Netflix remains active.
