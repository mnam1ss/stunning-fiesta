**Royal Family Issues Statement Following Duke's Arrest**

Buckingham Palace has released a statement on behalf of King Charles III following the arrest of his brother, Prince Andrew, by UK authorities. The statement confirms that the Royal Family is fully cooperating with the ongoing legal process. It expresses the family's support for the investigative bodies handling the case.

The arrest is connected to a long-running investigation into financial and business dealings. Prince Andrew has not been charged with any offense at this time and has previously denied any wrongdoing related to the matters under scrutiny. Legal experts note that the development marks a significant escalation in the probe.

**Key points:**
*   King Charles has stated the Royal Family is cooperating with authorities.
*   Prince Andrew was arrested as part of an investigation into his finances.
*   He has not been formally charged and maintains his innocence.
