A couple staying at a hotel in Shenzhen, China, discovered that their private moments had been recorded and distributed online without their knowledge. The incident, which involved a hidden camera, was reported to authorities after the individuals found footage of themselves on a website.

Local police have confirmed they are investigating the case. The hotel management stated they are cooperating fully with the investigation and are conducting their own internal review of security protocols. This event highlights ongoing concerns about the illicit use of spy cameras in private accommodations.

Key points:
- A hotel stay in Shenzhen resulted in a couple's intimate moments being secretly filmed.
- Police are investigating, and the hotel is reviewing its security measures.
- The case underscores broader issues of privacy invasion via hidden recording devices.
