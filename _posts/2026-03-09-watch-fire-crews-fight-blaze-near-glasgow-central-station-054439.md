Fire crews are responding to a significant fire near Glasgow Central Station. The blaze has led to the collapse of a dome on a building adjacent to the station.

ScotRail has announced that no train services will run in or out of Glasgow Central Station on Monday due to the incident. The disruption is expected to affect travel throughout the day as emergency services continue their work at the scene. Authorities have not yet released information regarding the cause of the fire or any potential injuries.

The situation is ongoing, and commuters are advised to seek alternative travel arrangements and check for further updates from rail operators.

Key points:
• A major fire near Glasgow Central Station caused the collapse of a neighbouring building's dome.
• ScotRail has cancelled all train services at the station for Monday.
• The cause of the fire and information on casualties is not yet available.
