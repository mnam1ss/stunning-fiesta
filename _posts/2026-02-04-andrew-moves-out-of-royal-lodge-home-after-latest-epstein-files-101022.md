The Duke of York had been residing at the 30-room Royal Lodge under a lease agreement with the Crown Estate.

A spokesperson for the prince confirmed the change in residence but did not comment on the specific reasons behind the timing of the move. The decision is reported to have been made in consultation with King Charles III.
