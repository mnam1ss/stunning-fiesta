**Senior Russian Military Official Wounded in Moscow Shooting**

A high-ranking Russian military officer was seriously injured in a shooting incident in Moscow on Tuesday. The victim has been identified as Lieutenant General Vladimir Alexeyev, a senior official within a key directorate of the nation's armed forces general staff.

According to initial reports from state-affiliated media, the attack occurred as the general was near his residence. He was transported to a hospital after sustaining multiple gunshot wounds. His current condition has not been officially disclosed. Law enforcement agencies have cordoned off the area and an investigation into the circumstances of the shooting is underway. No suspects have been immediately named, and the motive remains unclear.

Key points:
*   Lt Gen Vladimir Alexeyev, a senior figure in Russia's military staff, was shot multiple times.
*   The incident occurred in Moscow, and the general was hospitalized with serious injuries.
*   An investigation has been launched, with no immediate claim of responsibility or confirmed motive.
