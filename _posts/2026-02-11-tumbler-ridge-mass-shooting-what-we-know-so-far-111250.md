**Shooting in Western Canada Leaves Multiple Dead and Injured**

A series of shootings in the rural community of Tumbler Ridge, British Columbia, has resulted in multiple casualties. The incidents, which occurred at a school and a residential property, have left nine people dead and 25 others injured. Local authorities are currently on the scene, and the area has been secured as the investigation continues. Emergency services responded to reports of the violence, transporting the wounded to nearby medical facilities. Officials have not yet released information about a potential suspect or a motive for the attacks. The community, located in a remote region of western Canada, is receiving support from provincial and national law enforcement agencies. Further details are expected as the situation develops.

Key points:
- Nine fatalities and 25 injuries reported from shootings at two locations.
- Incidents occurred at a school and a home in Tumbler Ridge, British Columbia.
- Investigation is ongoing; no suspect or motive information has been released.
