The 2024 Brit Awards ceremony was held in Manchester on Saturday evening, with numerous music artists arriving for the event. Attendees on the red carpet included singer-songwriter Harry Styles, as well as performers Olivia Dean and Lola Young. International star Rosalía was also among those present at the annual UK music industry awards show. The ceremony, which honors achievements in British and international music, featured performances and award presentations throughout the evening. This year marked a notable change as the event was hosted outside of London for the first time in its history, taking place at the city's Co-op Live arena.

Key points:
- The Brit Awards ceremony was held in Manchester, a departure from its traditional London venue.
- Notable arrivals included Harry Styles, Olivia Dean, Lola Young, and Rosalía.
- The event recognizes annual achievements in both British and international music.
