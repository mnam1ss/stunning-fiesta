**UK Government Opens Booking Portal for Charter Flights from Dubai**

The UK government has launched a dedicated online portal for British nationals in Dubai to book seats on charter flights. This initiative follows widespread flight cancellations affecting the region over the past week, which have disrupted commercial air travel for many passengers.

Officials stated the portal is intended to assist citizens needing to return to the United Kingdom. The charter service is being organized in response to the ongoing operational challenges faced by several airlines in the Middle East. Travelers are directed to the official government website for booking instructions and eligibility details.

Key points:
*   A new online system allows Britons in Dubai to book government-chartered flights.
*   The action responds to significant flight cancellations across the Middle East.
*   The service aims to provide a return option for affected UK citizens.
