**Police Seek Information After Body Found in Bin**

Police have launched a public appeal for information after the body of an unidentified man was discovered inside a wheeled refuse container. Authorities are working to establish the man's identity, the circumstances of his death, and the location where the fatal incident occurred.

Initial reports indicate the man may have been struck by a vehicle prior to his body being placed in the bin. Investigators are treating the death as unexplained and are urging anyone with relevant details to come forward. The appeal emphasizes the need to piece together the man's final movements and the events leading to his death.

Key points:
*   An unidentified man's body was found in a wheelie bin.
*   Police are investigating the circumstances and location of his death.
*   Authorities believe he may have been hit by a car prior to being placed in the container.
