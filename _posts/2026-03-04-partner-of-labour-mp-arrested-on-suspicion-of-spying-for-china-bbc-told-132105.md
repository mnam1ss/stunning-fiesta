**Partner of Labour MP Arrested in National Security Investigation**

London police have arrested a man who is the partner of a serving Labour Party Member of Parliament on suspicion of espionage-related offenses. The arrest is part of a broader investigation into activities allegedly supporting Chinese intelligence services within the United Kingdom.

According to official sources, the individual is one of three men detained under the Official Secrets Act. The Metropolitan Police have stated the investigation centers on suspected breaches of national security. The Labour MP involved has not been publicly named in connection with the police operation. Authorities have emphasized that the investigation is ongoing and no charges have been filed at this time.

Key points:
*   The domestic partner of a Labour MP was arrested on suspicion of spying for China.
*   The arrest is linked to a wider probe into three individuals suspected of assisting Chinese intelligence.
*   The investigation is being conducted under the Official Secrets Act, with no charges yet brought.
