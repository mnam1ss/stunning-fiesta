Actress Jessie Buckley has stated that her involvement in acting during her teenage years was instrumental in managing an eating disorder. In a recent interview, the performer, who is currently considered a strong contender for major awards, described the craft as an essential and natural part of her life that supported her mental well-being.

Buckley explained that engaging with different characters provided a constructive outlet during a difficult period. She emphasized the therapeutic role her profession has played, allowing her to explore emotions and experiences outside of herself. The actress did not provide specific details about the disorder but highlighted the ongoing importance of artistic expression in her personal health.

Key points:
* Jessie Buckley credits acting with helping her cope with an eating disorder as a teenager.
* She describes the profession as a fundamental and therapeutic part of her life.
* Buckley is currently recognized as a leading nominee in the upcoming awards season.
