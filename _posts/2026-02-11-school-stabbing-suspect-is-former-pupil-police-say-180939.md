**Former Pupil Arrested After School Stabbing Incident**

Police in London have confirmed that a suspect arrested following a stabbing at a school is a former pupil of the institution. The incident occurred on Wednesday at a secondary school in the north-west of the city.

Two male students, aged 12 and 13, sustained injuries and were transported to a hospital. Medical officials report both boys are now in stable condition. Authorities have not released the identity of the arrested suspect, who remains in custody.

The school was temporarily secured following the attack, and police have stated there is no further threat to the public. An investigation into the circumstances surrounding the incident is ongoing.

Key points:
*   A former student has been arrested in connection with the stabbing.
*   Two young teenagers were injured and are hospitalized in stable condition.
*   Police confirm there is no ongoing threat to the wider community.
