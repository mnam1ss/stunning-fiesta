St Mary's Church in Woodbridge has raised a significant portion of its fundraising goal through an art raffle. The parish offered a chance to win an original painting by the renowned Suffolk artist Maggi Hambling, selling tickets for £50 each. The raffle concluded successfully, generating £22,000 in proceeds.

The funds are dedicated to a major renovation project for the historic church building. The primary objective is to install a new heating system, a necessary upgrade estimated to cost £200,000 in total. Church officials have stated that the current system is outdated and inefficient, making the building uncomfortable during colder months and posing challenges for community use.

This initiative is part of a broader campaign to secure the remaining funds needed for the heating installation and other essential maintenance work.

Key points:
• St Mary's Church in Woodbridge raised £22,000 by raffling a Maggi Hambling painting.
• The money supports a £200,000 project to install a new heating system.
• The church is continuing its fundraising efforts to meet the total cost.
