FBI officials have stated that a recent shooting at a Texas bar is being investigated as a potential act of terrorism. The incident resulted in multiple casualties, with two individuals confirmed deceased and over a dozen others wounded. Law enforcement reports indicate the suspected attacker was also killed at the scene. The investigation is ongoing as authorities work to determine the motive and any possible connections to broader extremist ideologies. No further details regarding the suspect or specific evidence have been released to the public at this time.

Key points:
- The FBI is investigating the Texas bar shooting as a potential terrorism case.
- The attack left two people dead and more than a dozen injured.
- The suspected gunman was killed by responding authorities.
