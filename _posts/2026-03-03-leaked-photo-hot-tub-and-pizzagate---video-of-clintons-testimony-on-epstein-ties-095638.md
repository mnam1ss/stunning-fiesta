Newly released video footage shows former President Bill Clinton and former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton giving separate depositions regarding their past associations with the late financier Jeffrey Epstein. The videos, made public as part of a court-ordered disclosure, capture the Clintons answering questions under oath. The release has drawn renewed public attention to the long-running case and the prominent figures connected to Epstein.

During the proceedings, topics reportedly included the nature and frequency of the Clintons' contacts with Epstein, who died in jail in 2019 while awaiting trial on sex trafficking charges. The footage does not contain major new allegations against the couple but provides a visual record of their sworn testimony. The deposition videos have circulated online, where they have been discussed alongside various unrelated conspiracy theories.

Key points:
• Video depositions of Bill and Hillary Clinton discussing Jeffrey Epstein have been publicly released.
• The footage shows the former officials answering questions under oath about their associations.
• Epstein was a financier who died in custody while charged with sex trafficking.
