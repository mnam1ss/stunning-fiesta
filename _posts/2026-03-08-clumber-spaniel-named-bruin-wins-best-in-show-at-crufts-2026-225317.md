A Clumber spaniel named Bruin has been awarded the title of Best in Show at the 2026 Crufts dog show. The prestigious competition concluded with the final judging at the National Exhibition Centre in Birmingham.

Bruin’s handler, Lee Cox, accepted the trophy and described the winning dog in highly personal terms, noting the animal's exceptional qualities. The victory marks a significant achievement for the breed, which is known for its distinctive appearance and steady temperament.

The annual Crufts event, organized by The Kennel Club, features thousands of dogs competing across various categories before one is selected as the overall champion.

• Bruin, a Clumber spaniel, won Best in Show at Crufts 2026.

• The win brings prominent recognition to the Clumber spaniel breed.
