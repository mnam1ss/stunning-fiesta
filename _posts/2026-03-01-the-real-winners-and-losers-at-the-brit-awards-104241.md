The 2024 Brit Awards ceremony, held in London, featured a mix of expected victories and notable public moments. The evening celebrated a diverse range of musical talent, with several first-time winners taking home trophies. Performances from established and emerging artists punctuated the award presentations, which were broadcast live to a national audience.
