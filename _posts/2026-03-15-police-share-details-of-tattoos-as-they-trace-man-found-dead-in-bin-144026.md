Police in the city have released a detailed description of a deceased man’s tattoos in an effort to establish his identity. The man was discovered deceased in a commercial waste bin earlier this week.

Authorities stated that the investigation is ongoing and no further details about the circumstances are being released at this time. The public appeal focuses specifically on the unique body art, which includes several prominent designs. Investigators hope that the descriptions will prompt recognition from someone who may know the individual.

Forensic work and a post-mortem examination are continuing as part of the standard procedure. Police have asked anyone with relevant information to contact them.

Key points:
- Police are seeking help to identify a man found deceased.
- Distinctive tattoo descriptions have been released to the public.
- The investigation remains active, with authorities urging anyone with information to come forward.
