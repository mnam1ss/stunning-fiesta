Pop star Harry Styles and boxer Anthony Joshua have been named among the United Kingdom's highest individual taxpayers, according to a new ranking. The annual list, compiled by *The Sunday Times*, details the top 50 taxpayers based on analysis of their publicly available financial contributions.

Styles, who first rose to fame as a member of the band One Direction before launching a successful solo career, appears on the list for the first time. Heavyweight boxing champion Anthony Joshua is also a prominent name in the rankings. The list estimates the total tax paid by these individuals on income, capital gains, and other applicable duties over the past year.

The publication of the list often sparks public discussion about the tax contributions of high-earning celebrities and business figures.
