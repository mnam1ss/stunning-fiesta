Satellite imagery analyzed by BBC Verify indicates that recent military strikes, reportedly conducted by the United States and Israel, have resulted in significant damage to Iranian military assets. The images show that at least eleven naval vessels belonging to Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps Navy have been destroyed. The strikes also reportedly targeted facilities associated with Iran's nuclear program, though the extent of that damage is less clear from the available imagery. The reported operations have not been officially acknowledged by the U.S. or Israeli governments. The incident marks a notable escalation in regional tensions, occurring amid ongoing international concerns over Iran's military and nuclear activities.

Key points:
• Satellite analysis confirms the destruction of over ten Iranian naval vessels.
• Strikes also reportedly targeted sites linked to Iran's nuclear program.
• The actions have not been formally claimed by the U.S. or Israeli governments.
