**Iranian Public Expresses Concern Over Conflict's Aftermath**

A recent report highlights growing apprehension among some Iranian citizens who initially supported military action. Interviews conducted with individuals from various professions, including education and commerce, reveal a shift in sentiment. These individuals now express concern that prolonged conflict could lead to severe instability within the country.

The report indicates that initial support is being tempered by fears of potential societal and economic collapse. Citizens are reportedly questioning the long-term consequences of ongoing hostilities, worrying about the future state of the nation.

Key points:
*   Some Iranians who backed military action are now voicing doubts.
*   Concerns center on the risk of national chaos and ruin from extended conflict.
*   Professionals from different sectors are among those expressing these anxieties.
