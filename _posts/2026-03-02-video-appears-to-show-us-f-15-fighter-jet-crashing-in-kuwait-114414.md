A video circulating online reportedly shows a U.S. Air Force F-15 fighter jet crashing in Kuwait. According to a U.S. statement, the incident involved six F-15 aircraft that were unintentionally engaged by Kuwaiti air defense systems. The statement confirmed that all crew members involved are currently in stable condition. The footage, which has not been independently verified, shows an aircraft descending rapidly with a trail of smoke before impacting the ground. Authorities from both nations are expected to conduct a joint investigation into the circumstances of the engagement. The incident highlights the complexities of operating integrated air defense networks during multinational military exercises or operations in the region.

Key points:
• A video purports to show a U.S. F-15 jet crash in Kuwait following an engagement by local air defenses.
• U.S. officials state six jets were involved and all crew members are reported stable.
• An investigation into the mistaken engagement is anticipated.
