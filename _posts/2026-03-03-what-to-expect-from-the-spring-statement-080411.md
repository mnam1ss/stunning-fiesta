Chancellor of the Exchequer Rachel Reeves is scheduled to deliver a significant fiscal update to Parliament on 3 March. The event, known as the Spring Statement, will provide a detailed assessment of the current state of the UK economy and outline the government's financial plans.

The statement will be accompanied by the latest official economic forecasts from the Office for Budget Responsibility. These independent projections are expected to cover key indicators such as growth, inflation, and public borrowing. The address is anticipated to set the government's fiscal direction ahead of the next general election.

Key points:
*   Chancellor Rachel Reeves will present the Spring Statement on 3 March.
*   The update will include the Office for Budget Responsibility's economic forecast.
*   The statement is expected to detail the government's economic strategy and fiscal plans.
