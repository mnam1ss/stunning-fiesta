**Former Royal Released Pending Further Inquiry**

A former senior royal has been released from police custody while an investigation continues into allegations of misconduct in a public role. The individual was detained earlier on Thursday and was seen departing a police station that evening. Law enforcement officials have confirmed that related search operations at a residence in Norfolk have now concluded. The investigation remains active, and no formal charges have been filed at this time. The case is being handled by a specialized unit within the police force.

Key points:
*   A former royal was arrested on suspicion of misconduct in public office and later released under investigation.
*   Police have finished conducting searches at a property in Norfolk connected to the inquiry.
*   The investigation is ongoing, and no charges have yet been brought.
