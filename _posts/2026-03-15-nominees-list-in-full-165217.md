The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences has announced the nominees for this year's Academy Awards. Among the films recognized across multiple categories are several prominent titles that have garnered significant attention throughout the awards season. The historical drama *One Battle After Another* and the literary adaptation *Hamnet* are featured prominently in the list. The crime thriller *Sinners* also secured several key nominations. The full roster of contenders spans twenty-three categories, including Best Picture, Directing, and all four acting awards. The announcement sets the stage for the annual ceremony, which honors outstanding achievements in filmmaking from the preceding year. Industry analysts will now scrutinize the list as a predictor for the final awards distribution.

Key points:
* The Academy has released the official list of nominees for the upcoming Oscars ceremony.
* Major nominated films include *One Battle After Another*, *Sinners*, and *Hamnet*.
* Nominations cover all major categories, from Best Picture to the acting awards.
