**Teenager Detained Following Stabbing Incident at School**

A 16-year-old boy has been taken into custody by police after a girl was stabbed at a school in England. The incident occurred during the school day, causing significant disruption and alarm. According to reports, students and staff were instructed to take emergency shelter when the violence broke out. The injured girl was treated at the scene before being transported to a hospital; her current condition has not been officially disclosed. Police have confirmed the arrest of a teenage male on suspicion of attempted murder and stated that they are not seeking any other individuals in connection with the event. An investigation is ongoing, with authorities working to establish the full circumstances surrounding the attack.

Key points:
- A 16-year-old boy is in custody after a girl was stabbed at a school.
- The injured girl received medical treatment and was taken to a hospital.
- Police have stated the incident is isolated and no other suspects are being sought.
