**Texas Voters Weigh In on Middle East Strikes Ahead of Primaries**

With the Texas primary elections approaching, some voters supporting former President Donald Trump have shared their views on recent U.S. military strikes against Iranian-linked targets. Interviews conducted in the state reveal a range of perspectives on the international situation as domestic political contests unfold.

Several supporters expressed strong approval of a forceful military response, linking the action to broader themes of national strength. Others voiced concern about potential escalation, emphasizing a desire to avoid prolonged foreign engagements. The timing of the strikes, just before the primary vote, has also led some to discuss the interplay between international events and domestic political priorities.

Key points:
*   Some Texas Trump supporters endorse the strikes as a demonstration of U.S. resolve.
*   Concerns were raised by others regarding the risk of a wider regional conflict.
*   The geopolitical developments are being considered by voters alongside immediate electoral choices.
