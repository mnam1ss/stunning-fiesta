The Princess of Wales has released a video message in honor of St David’s Day, the national day of Wales. In the recording, she delivered her remarks entirely in the Welsh language. The princess expressed a strong personal and familial connection to the nation, stating that it holds significant meaning for her and her family.

The video was published on official social media channels. St David’s Day is celebrated annually on March 1st to commemorate the patron saint of Wales, often featuring displays of daffodils and leeks, the national symbols.

Key points:
* The Princess of Wales issued a special video message for St David’s Day.
* The address was conducted fully in Welsh, marking a notable public communication.
* She emphasized the deep personal significance Wales holds for the royal family.
