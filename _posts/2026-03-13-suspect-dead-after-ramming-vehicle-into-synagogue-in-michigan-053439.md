**Suspect Dies Following Vehicle Attack at Michigan Synagogue**

A suspect is deceased after driving a vehicle into a synagogue in Michigan, according to authorities. The Federal Bureau of Investigation stated the event is under investigation as a deliberate act of violence targeting the Jewish community. The incident occurred on Sunday, though the exact location within the state was not immediately specified in initial reports.

Emergency services responded to the scene. There were no immediate reports of injuries to anyone other than the suspect. Law enforcement officials have not released the identity of the deceased individual or a possible motive. The investigation remains active, with the FBI leading the effort to determine the full circumstances surrounding the attack.

Key points:
*   A suspect died after ramming a vehicle into a Michigan synagogue.
*   The FBI is investigating the incident as a targeted attack on the Jewish community.
*   No other injuries have been reported; the investigation is ongoing.
