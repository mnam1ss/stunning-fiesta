A 43-year-old woman has been charged with murder following the death of her newborn child in central London. Zahira Byjaouane was arrested after her 18-day-old infant sustained fatal injuries from a fall from height earlier this month. Emergency services responded to the incident, but the baby was pronounced dead at the scene.

The Metropolitan Police have confirmed that Byjaouane has been remanded in custody and is scheduled to appear before a magistrates' court. The investigation into the precise circumstances surrounding the infant's death remains active, with authorities continuing their inquiries. No further details about the location or specific events leading to the fall have been released by officials at this time.

Key points:
• A mother has been charged with murder after her 18-day-old baby died from a fall in London.
• Zahira Byjaouane, 43, is in custody and awaiting a court appearance.
• Police are continuing their investigation into the incident.
