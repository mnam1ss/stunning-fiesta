Iranian military forces have conducted strikes targeting a location in Bahrain, according to statements from Iranian officials. The reported target is in the vicinity of a major U.S. military facility in the capital, Manama. Visual reports from the area indicated significant smoke rising near the installation that serves as the base for the U.S. Navy's Fifth Fleet.

This action is part of a broader series of launches announced by Iran across the region. The Iranian government has stated the operations are a response to what it describes as hostile activities. There has been no immediate independent confirmation of damage or casualties from the incident in Bahrain. U.S. military authorities have not yet issued a formal statement regarding the reported event.

Key points:
*   Iranian officials state they have struck near a U.S. facility in Manama, Bahrain.
*   The incident is reportedly part of wider Iranian military strikes across the region.
*   Damage assessments and official U.S. response are pending.
