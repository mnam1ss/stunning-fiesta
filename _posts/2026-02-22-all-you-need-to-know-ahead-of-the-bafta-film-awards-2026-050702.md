The 2026 British Academy Film Awards ceremony will be held this Sunday in London, marking a key event in the international film industry's awards season. The ceremony honors outstanding achievements in both British and international cinema from the past year.

A full list of nominees across all categories has been published by BAFTA. The event will be broadcast live on several television networks and will also be available for streaming. Coverage typically includes a red carpet arrivals segment followed by the main awards presentation.

**Key points:**
*   The BAFTA Film Awards for 2026 are scheduled to take place in London this weekend.
*   Nominations have been announced, with the ceremony set to be televised and streamed live.
*   The awards recognize excellence in filmmaking, serving as a precursor to other major industry awards.
