London Fashion Week has commenced its 2026 edition, featuring presentations from 90 designers. Organizers have stated their aim for this year's event to be the largest in its history, based on the number of participating brands and expected global attendance. The schedule includes runway shows and presentations across multiple venues in the city.

Industry professionals and buyers from around the world are attending the event, which serves as a key platform for both established and emerging designers to showcase their upcoming collections. The week's activities are anticipated to set trends for the forthcoming fashion season.

Key points:
*   The 2026 event involves 90 different designer brands.
*   Organizers have expressed a goal for it to become the most extensive iteration of the fashion week to date.
*   The event functions as a major international platform for presenting new seasonal collections.
