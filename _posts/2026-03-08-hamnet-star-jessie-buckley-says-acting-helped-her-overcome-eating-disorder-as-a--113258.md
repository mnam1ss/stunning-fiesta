**Actor Credits Acting with Supporting Mental Health in Youth**

In a recent interview, actor Jessie Buckley discussed how her involvement in acting during her teenage years provided significant support for her mental well-being. Buckley, who is currently receiving acclaim for her performance in the film *Hamnet*, described the craft as an essential and natural part of her life.

The actor revealed that she struggled with an eating disorder as an adolescent. She stated that engaging with acting roles offered a constructive outlet and a means of processing her experiences during that challenging period. Buckley emphasized the personal importance of her work, suggesting it serves a fundamental role beyond professional achievement.
