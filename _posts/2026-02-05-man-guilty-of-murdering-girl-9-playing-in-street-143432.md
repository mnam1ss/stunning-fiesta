A man has been found guilty of murdering a nine-year-old girl in a public street in Lincolnshire. The incident occurred in the town of Boston in July 2022, when the child was fatally attacked while playing outside a business premises. Following a trial at Nottingham Crown Court, a jury convicted the defendant of murder. The court heard that the victim, Lilia Valutyte, died from stab wounds. Sentencing for the convicted individual is scheduled for a later date. The investigation was led by Lincolnshire Police, who described the case as a deeply tragic event for the local community and the girl's family.

Key points:
• A man was convicted of murdering nine-year-old Lilia Valutyte in Boston, Lincolnshire.
• The fatal stabbing occurred while the child was playing outside a shop in July 2022.
• Sentencing will occur at a future hearing following the guilty verdict.
