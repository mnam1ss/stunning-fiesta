**Boy Charged Following School Stabbing Incident in Norfolk**

A teenage boy has been formally charged in connection with the stabbing of a girl at a school in Norfolk. The incident, which occurred on school grounds, prompted a significant emergency response. Armed police units were deployed to the location and were seen entering the school building as part of their operation.

The injured teenage girl received immediate medical attention at the scene before being transported to a hospital. Authorities have confirmed her condition is not believed to be life-threatening. The suspect, who cannot be named due to his age, is scheduled to appear before a youth court.

The police have stated that this is being treated as an isolated incident, with no wider threat to the public. An investigation into the circumstances surrounding the attack is ongoing.

Key points:
*   A teenage boy has been charged after a girl was stabbed at a Norfolk school.
*   Armed police responded to the incident, which is described as isolated.
*   The victim's injuries are not considered life-threatening.
