At least 148 people were killed in a reported strike on a school in Iran, according to Iranian officials. The incident has drawn international attention and conflicting statements from involved nations. Iranian authorities have publicly attributed responsibility for the attack to the United States and Israel. In response, a U.S. spokesperson stated that American officials are currently reviewing reports of the event to verify the details. Meanwhile, the Israel Defense Forces issued a statement indicating they have no knowledge of any military actions being conducted in the specific area where the strike occurred. The situation remains under investigation as conflicting narratives emerge from the different parties.

Key points:
- Iranian officials report a strike on a school resulting in significant casualties.
- Iran has accused both the United States and Israel of carrying out the attack.
- The U.S. is investigating the reports, while Israel denies awareness of operations there.
