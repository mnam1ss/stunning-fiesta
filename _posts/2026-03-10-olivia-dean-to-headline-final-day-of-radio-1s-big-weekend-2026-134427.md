Olivia Dean has been announced as the headline performer for the closing day of BBC Radio 1's Big Weekend festival in 2026. The event is scheduled to be held in Sunderland, marking a return to the North East of England. The three-day festival will occupy Herrington Country Park from the 22nd to the 24th of May.

The booking represents a significant platform for the BRIT Award-winning artist, who is known for her soul-infused pop music. The festival is a major fixture in the UK's live music calendar, typically featuring a diverse lineup of popular and emerging acts across multiple stages. Further details regarding the full artist roster and ticket release dates are expected to be announced in the coming months.

Key points:
* Olivia Dean will headline the final day of Radio 1's Big Weekend in May 2026.
* The festival is set to take place at Herrington Country Park in Sunderland.
* The event is scheduled for three days, from 22 to 24 May.
