Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky has stated that the United States is aiming for a resolution to the conflict with Russia by June, according to recent comments. Zelensky indicated that significant challenges persist in negotiations, even as this reported timeline is discussed. The statement comes amid continued Russian military actions, which have recently targeted Ukrainian energy infrastructure with new strikes.

The Ukrainian leader did not specify the origin of the June timeframe within the U.S. government. He emphasized that complex diplomatic issues remain unresolved between the warring nations. Military engagements have not ceased, with the latest attacks focusing on power generation and distribution facilities.

Key points:
* President Zelensky cites a U.S. objective to end the war by June.
* He notes that substantial diplomatic hurdles continue to exist.
* Russian forces are conducting ongoing strikes against Ukrainian energy sites.
