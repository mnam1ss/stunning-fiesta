**Cold Snap Arrives, Duration Under Review**

A marked drop in temperatures was felt across many regions on Friday, signaling a shift from recent milder conditions. Meteorologists attribute this change to a shift in wind patterns, now drawing colder air from the north and east. This has replaced the previously dominant, warmer airflow.

Forecasters are currently analyzing weather models to determine how long these chillier conditions will persist. While some indications suggest the cold spell may be relatively brief, the exact timeline remains uncertain. The public is advised to consult updated local forecasts for specific details on temperature trends and any potential weather warnings in the coming days.

Key points:
*   A significant temperature drop occurred Friday due to a change in wind direction.
*   The colder air is currently sourced from northern and eastern regions.
*   The duration of this cold spell is still being assessed by forecasters.
