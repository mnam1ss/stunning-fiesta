Prince William has expressed his condolences following the death of a 25-year-old British Army medic who gained popularity on social media. The soldier, identified as Lance Corporal Lucy Wilde, was found deceased at her barracks earlier this week.

In a statement released by Kensington Palace, the Prince of Wales described feeling profound sorrow upon learning the news. He extended his deepest sympathies to her family, friends, and fellow service members, acknowledging her service to the country. The circumstances surrounding her death are not yet public, and investigations by the relevant authorities are ongoing.

Lance Corporal Wilde was known to a wider audience through her TikTok account, where she shared insights into military life. Her content, which often featured lighthearted moments from her training and duties, had attracted a significant following.

Key points:
- Prince William has issued a statement of condolence after the death of Army medic Lucy Wilde.
- The 25-year-old soldier was found dead at her barracks; the circumstances are under investigation.
- Wilde was known for her TikTok presence, where she posted about her experiences in the military.
