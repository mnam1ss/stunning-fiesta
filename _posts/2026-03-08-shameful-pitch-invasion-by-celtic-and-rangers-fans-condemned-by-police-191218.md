Police have condemned a pitch invasion by supporters following a football match between Celtic and Rangers. The incident occurred after Celtic secured a 4-2 victory in a penalty shootout. Authorities reported that multiple arrests were made during the subsequent clashes between fans and officers. Injuries were sustained by both police personnel and spectators in the disorder. Officials described the crowd's actions as unacceptable and a breach of stadium safety protocols. An investigation into the events is ongoing.

Key points:
• Multiple arrests were made following a pitch invasion after a Celtic victory.
• Injuries were reported among both police officers and fans.
• Authorities have condemned the behavior and are investigating the incident.
