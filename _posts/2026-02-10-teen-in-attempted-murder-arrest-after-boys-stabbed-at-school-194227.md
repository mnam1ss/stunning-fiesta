**Teenager Arrested Following School Stabbing Incident**

A teenager has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder after two boys were seriously injured in a stabbing at a school. The incident occurred on school grounds, prompting a significant emergency response. Law enforcement officials have confirmed that counter-terrorism police are now leading the investigation into the attack. The two victims, both students, were transported to a hospital with serious injuries and are currently receiving medical treatment. Authorities have not released the identities of those involved, citing the ongoing nature of the inquiry. The school has been closed as police continue their forensic examination of the scene. Community leaders have urged calm while the investigation proceeds.

**Key points:**
*   A teenage suspect is in custody on attempted murder charges.
*   Counter-terrorism police are directing the investigation.
*   Two students sustained serious injuries and are hospitalized.
