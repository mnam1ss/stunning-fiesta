**Prince Andrew Arrested on Suspicion of Misconduct in Public Office**

London police have confirmed the arrest of Prince Andrew, the Duke of York, on suspicion of misconduct in a public office. The arrest occurred earlier today, with the prince taken into custody for questioning. The specific allegations prompting the arrest have not been publicly detailed by authorities at this time.

A spokesperson for the Metropolitan Police stated the arrest is part of an ongoing investigation. Prince Andrew was later released on bail pending further inquiries. Buckingham Palace has not yet issued an official statement regarding the development. The arrest marks a significant legal development for a senior member of the Royal Family.

Key points:
*   Prince Andrew was arrested by London police on suspicion of misconduct in a public office.
*   He has been released on bail as the investigation continues.
*   The specific allegations underlying the arrest have not been publicly disclosed.
