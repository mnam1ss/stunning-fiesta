Former US President Donald Trump has commented on the UK's decision regarding a recent US military operation, drawing a comparison to a former British leader. The remarks followed reports that UK Prime Minister Sir Keir Starmer declined a US request to utilize the Diego Garcia military base for strikes against Iran.

The base, a British overseas territory in the Indian Ocean, is often used as a strategic logistics hub by US forces. The reported refusal pertains to US plans for retaliatory strikes following attacks on American troops. In his statement, Trump contrasted Starmer's approach with the legacy of Winston Churchill, suggesting the current prime minister's stance differed from the historical alliance.

The UK government has not publicly detailed the specifics of the request or its decision-making process. The incident highlights ongoing diplomatic and military coordination between the two allied nations.

*   Former President Trump commented on the UK's reported refusal of a US request to use the Diego Garcia base for strikes on Iran.
*   Trump contrasted Prime Minister Starmer's decision with the historical leadership of Winston Churchill.
