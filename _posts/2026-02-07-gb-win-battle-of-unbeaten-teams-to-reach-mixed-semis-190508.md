Team Great Britain has secured a spot in the mixed doubles curling semi-finals at the Winter Olympics. The team advanced with two round-robin matches still remaining on the schedule after defeating both Canada and the United States in consecutive games. These wins preserved the British duo's undefeated record in the tournament so far.

The victories came against two teams considered among the strongest contenders in the competition. By winning these matches, the British pair guaranteed themselves a top-two finish in their group, which is sufficient to progress to the medal round. The team's performance has positioned them favorably as the knockout stages approach.

Key points:
- Great Britain's mixed doubles curling team is through to the Olympic semi-finals.
- The team remains undefeated after wins over Canada and the United States.
- Qualification was secured with two round-robin games left to play.
