Russian authorities have identified three individuals as suspects in the shooting of a high-ranking military officer. The incident, which targeted a Russian general, is currently under investigation. Officials state that one of the named suspects is believed to be the primary assailant. This individual is reported to have departed Russia following the attack, with authorities indicating his current location is the United Arab Emirates. The announcement marks a development in the case, though specific details regarding the other two suspects or the exact circumstances of the shooting have not been fully disclosed. The investigation into the attack continues as Russian officials pursue the suspects.

Key points:
- Russian officials have named three suspects in the shooting of a general.
- The alleged gunman is reported to have fled to the UAE after the incident.
- The investigation into the attack is ongoing.
