Paris Fashion Week concluded with a focus on celebrity appearances and emerging style trends. Among the notable attendees was actress Zendaya, who was photographed wearing a gold band on her left ring finger while attending shows. Her presence was part of a wider gathering of high-profile figures at the event.

The fashion presentations themselves highlighted specific themes, with designer Stella McCartney’s collection prominently featuring equestrian-inspired elements and motifs. The week served as a platform for showcasing new seasonal directions from numerous luxury fashion houses, drawing international attention to both the designs on the runway and the style choices of guests in the front rows.

Key points:
- Actress Zendaya attended Paris Fashion Week wearing a gold band on her left ring finger.
- Stella McCartney’s collection incorporated horse-related themes and imagery.
- The event featured the latest collections from major designers alongside numerous celebrity guests.
