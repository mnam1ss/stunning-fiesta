The first full day of competition at the 2026 Milan-Cortina Winter Olympics will feature five gold medal events this Saturday. Among the highlights is the debut of the men's big air snowboarding event on the Olympic program, which is expected to draw significant attention. The day's schedule also includes medal events in other disciplines, setting a competitive tone for the initial phase of the Games. Athletes from various nations will be contending for podium positions across these events, marking the start of the two-week international sporting competition in Italy.

Key points:
• Five gold medals will be awarded across different sports on the first Saturday.
• The men's big air snowboarding event makes its Olympic debut.
• The day establishes the initial medal standings for the 2026 Winter Olympics.
