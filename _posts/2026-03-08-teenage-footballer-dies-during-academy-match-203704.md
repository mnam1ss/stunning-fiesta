**Teenage Footballer Dies After Collapsing During Academy Match**

A 15-year-old academy footballer has died after collapsing during a youth match on Saturday. The incident occurred while the teenager was playing for Oxford United's youth side in a fixture against Fulham.

Emergency services were called to the scene and the player was treated by medical personnel before being transported to a hospital. He was later pronounced dead. The identity of the young footballer has not been publicly released at the request of his family.

Both clubs have issued statements expressing their profound sadness and offering condolences to the player's family and friends. The Football Association has been informed and is providing support. The match was abandoned following the incident.

**Key points:**
*   A 15-year-old Oxford United academy player died after collapsing on the pitch during a youth match against Fulham.
*   The incident occurred on Saturday, with the player receiving emergency treatment before being pronounced dead in hospital.
*   Both clubs and the Football Association have been notified, with the match being abandoned and an outpouring of support for the family.
