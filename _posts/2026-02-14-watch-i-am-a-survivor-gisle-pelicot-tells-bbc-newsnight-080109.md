**Woman Shares Account of Partner's Alleged Crimes in BBC Interview**

In a recent interview with BBC Newsnight, Gisèle Pelicot described her experience of learning about serious criminal allegations against her former partner. She stated that she found it difficult to reconcile the accusations with the person she knew. The interview was part of a segment focusing on the impact of crime on families and relationships.

Pelicot, who identified herself as a survivor, discussed the personal turmoil following the revelations about the man she was once close to. The broadcast included her reflections on the process of coping with the situation and its aftermath. The full interview is available through the BBC's online platforms.

Key points:
*   Gisèle Pelicot gave an interview to BBC Newsnight regarding allegations against her former partner.
*   She expressed difficulty believing the accused individual was capable of the alleged acts.
*   The segment examined the personal impact on those close to individuals accused of serious crimes.
