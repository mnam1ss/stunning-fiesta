**Retaliatory Strikes Continue as US Reports Military Casualties**

Military actions between the United States, Israel, and Iran have entered a second day, with reports of continued retaliatory strikes. The ongoing conflict has resulted in the deaths of three American military personnel, according to US officials. Former US President Donald Trump issued a public statement following the reported casualties, cautioning that further American losses could occur.

The operations, which began yesterday, mark a significant escalation in regional tensions. The exact locations and full scope of the current military engagements have not been independently verified. The situation remains fluid, with international observers closely monitoring developments for signs of further escalation or diplomatic intervention.

Key points:
*   A second day of military action involving US, Israeli, and Iranian forces is underway.
*   Three US service members have been reported killed during these operations.
*   Former President Donald Trump has warned of potential additional American fatalities.
