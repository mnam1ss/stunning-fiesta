A photograph has surfaced that appears to depict the Duke of York, Lord Peter Mandelson, and the late financier Jeffrey Epstein together. The image, published by the BBC, shows the three men seated outdoors on wooden decking, with the Duke and Lord Mandelson wearing bathrobes.

The photograph's origin and the exact circumstances of when it was taken have not been independently verified. A Buckingham Palace spokesperson has stated they do not comment on the Duke's private photographs. Lord Mandelson's office has been contacted for comment.

The emergence of the image renews public attention on the associations between prominent figures and Epstein, who died in jail in 2019 while awaiting trial on sex trafficking charges. The Duke of York settled a civil sexual assault case in the United States in 2022, without admission of liability.
