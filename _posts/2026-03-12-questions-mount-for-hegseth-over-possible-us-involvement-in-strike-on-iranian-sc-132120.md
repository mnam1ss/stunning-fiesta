**Lawmakers Seek Clarity on US Role in Reported Iran Strike**

Democratic members of Congress have formally requested information from Defense Secretary Lloyd Austin regarding potential U.S. involvement in a reported military strike on a facility in Iran. The incident, which Iranian state media described as an attack on a school, remains shrouded in uncertainty.

In response to the congressional inquiry, Secretary Austin stated that the matter is currently under investigation by the Department of Defense. He did not provide further details or confirm any American role in the event. The request from lawmakers highlights ongoing scrutiny of military operations in the region and a push for transparency following conflicting initial reports about the nature and location of the strike.

Key points:
*   U.S. lawmakers have asked the Defense Secretary for details on possible American involvement in a reported strike in Iran.
*   The Defense Secretary confirmed an investigation is underway but offered no confirmation of U.S. actions.
*   The inquiry follows Iranian claims that a school was hit, though the exact circumstances remain unclear.
