Freestyle skier Zoe Atkin has earned a bronze medal in the women's halfpipe event at the 2026 Winter Olympics. Her performance in the finals contributed a third-place finish to the British team's overall tally.

This medal marks the fifth for Great Britain at these Games. The achievement matches the nation's previous record for total medals won at a single Winter Olympics, set at earlier competitions.

The result underscores a successful campaign for Team GB in Milan-Cortina, with athletes securing podium finishes across multiple disciplines. Official celebrations are expected to continue as the Games proceed.

Key points:
• Zoe Atkin won bronze in the women's freestyle skiing halfpipe.
• This is Great Britain's fifth medal at the 2026 Winter Olympics.
• The total equals Team GB's record medal haul from a prior Winter Games.
