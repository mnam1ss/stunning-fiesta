FBI officials have stated that a recent shooting at a bar in Texas is being investigated as a potential act of terrorism. The incident resulted in multiple casualties, with two individuals confirmed deceased and over a dozen others wounded. Law enforcement reports indicate the suspected attacker was also killed during the event.

Authorities are examining the circumstances and motives behind the violence. The investigation remains active, with federal and local agencies collaborating to gather evidence. Officials have not released detailed information about the suspect or any specific ideological motivations at this stage.

The statement from the FBI underscores the ongoing assessment of the attack's nature, moving beyond initial reports of the violence to consider its broader context.

Key points:
• The FBI is investigating the Texas bar shooting as a potential terrorism case.
• The attack resulted in two fatalities and more than a dozen injuries.
• The suspected gunman was also killed, according to law enforcement.
