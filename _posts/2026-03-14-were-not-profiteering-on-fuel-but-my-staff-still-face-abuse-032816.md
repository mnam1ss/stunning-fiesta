Independent fuel retailer Goran Raven has publicly addressed the challenges facing his business amid sustained high oil prices, stating that his company is not taking excessive profits from the current market conditions. He described the elevated cost of wholesale fuel as a significant burden for his operation.

Raven emphasized that the financial pressure from sourcing fuel is severe for independent retailers, not just consumers. Despite this, he reported that his staff members continue to experience verbal abuse from some customers who are frustrated by prices at the pump. The retailer's comments highlight the difficult position of smaller operators caught between global oil markets and public sentiment.

Key points:
* An independent fuel retailer states his business is not profiteering from high prices.
* He says the wholesale oil market is creating severe pressure for his company.
* Staff at his outlets have faced customer abuse due to frustration over fuel costs.
