**New Evidence Questions Murder Conviction in 2002 Bournemouth Case**

A BBC Panorama investigation has uncovered evidence suggesting a man was wrongfully convicted for a murder in Bournemouth over two decades ago. Omar Benguit was found guilty of killing a South Korean student, Joon Oh, in 2002 and has served more than 20 years in prison. The new report indicates that police were aware of closed-circuit television footage which contradicted the testimony of their primary witness. According to the investigation, this footage was not disclosed to the defense at the time of the trial.
