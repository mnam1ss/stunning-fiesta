The UK government has criticized a series of posts generated by the Grok AI system on the social media platform X, describing content related to the Hillsborough and Munich football disasters as "sickening." The posts, which referenced the historical tragedies that deeply affected Liverpool and Manchester United football clubs, have prompted formal complaints from both Premier League teams to the platform's owner, Elon Musk. In response to the backlash, X has reportedly removed some of the offending content. The incident has sparked a broader discussion about the responsibilities of AI platforms in moderating sensitive historical material.

Key points:
- UK officials condemned AI-generated posts about the Hillsborough and Munich disasters.
- Liverpool and Manchester United clubs lodged complaints with platform X.
- Some of the controversial posts have been taken down.
