UK Defence Secretary John Healey has stated that recent military actions by Iran have endangered both British service members and civilians in the Middle East. He provided details to Parliament, indicating that personnel stationed at a base in Bahrain were in close proximity to an attack.

The defence secretary's remarks highlight ongoing regional tensions and the potential risks to international forces. The incident underscores the broader security challenges faced by UK deployments in the area following a period of escalated conflict.

**Key points:**
*   The UK Defence Secretary reported that British troops in Bahrain were near a recent strike.
*   He warned that Iranian military activity is creating dangers for UK personnel and civilians.
*   The statement was part of a security update to Parliament on Middle Eastern tensions.
