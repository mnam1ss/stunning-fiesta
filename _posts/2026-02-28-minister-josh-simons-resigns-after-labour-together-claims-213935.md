Minister Josh Simons has resigned from his government position following allegations related to his previous role at a political think tank. The controversy centers on claims that the organization, which he formerly led, commissioned research into the personal backgrounds of several journalists.

The allegations were publicized by the group Labour Together. Mr. Simons has not publicly commented on the specific claims since his resignation was announced. His departure marks a sudden exit from the frontbench.

The government has acknowledged his resignation but has not provided further details on the circumstances surrounding it. The opposition has called for transparency regarding the allegations.

**Key points:**
*   Minister Josh Simons has resigned following claims about a think tank he previously ran.
*   The think tank is alleged to have commissioned research into journalists' backgrounds.
*   The allegations were brought forward by the group Labour Together.
