**Bystander Attempted to Intervene in Vape Shop Fire**

A member of the public attempted to tackle a fire at a vape shop before the blaze escalated. The individual, identified as Lamin Kongira, reported to BBC Scotland News that he was walking past the establishment when the shopkeeper emerged, urgently shouting about the fire.

According to the report, Kongira then made an effort to combat the flames himself before emergency services arrived. The fire subsequently developed into a more significant inferno. Firefighters were called to the scene to bring the situation under control. No immediate details were provided regarding the cause of the fire or potential injuries.

Key points:
- A passer-by, Lamin Kongira, attempted to fight an initial fire at a vape shop.
- The shopkeeper alerted him by running out of the store shouting about the fire.
- The incident escalated into a major blaze requiring a full emergency service response.
