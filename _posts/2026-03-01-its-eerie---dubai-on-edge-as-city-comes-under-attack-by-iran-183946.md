Dubai residents and visitors reported a tense atmosphere as the city faced a military strike from Iran. Witnesses described seeing smoke rising in the distance, creating a stark contrast with the city's usual bustling tourist and commercial activities. Some tourists noted the unsettling feeling of attempting to continue with leisure plans while aware of the nearby conflict.

Local authorities have urged calm and are assessing the situation. Flights at Dubai International Airport, a major global hub, have experienced disruptions and delays. The attack marks a significant escalation in regional tensions and has raised concerns over security in the typically stable emirate.

Key points:
*   Military action from Iran created visible smoke and disrupted normal life in Dubai.
*   Airport operations faced delays as authorities worked to ensure security.
*   The incident represents a notable increase in regional geopolitical tensions.
