A 15-year-old boy has been arrested following an incident at a secondary school in Pembrokeshire, Wales, which left a teacher injured. Authorities responded to Milford Haven Comprehensive School on Thursday afternoon after receiving reports of a disturbance.

Emergency services attended the scene, and the injured staff member was treated. The nature and extent of the teacher's injuries have not been officially disclosed. The suspect was taken into custody on suspicion of attempted murder and remains in police custody for questioning.

The school has confirmed the incident and stated it is cooperating fully with the ongoing police investigation. Officials have reassured parents and the local community that there is no wider threat to public safety.

Key points:
• A teacher was injured at Milford Haven Comprehensive School on Thursday.
• A 15-year-old male pupil has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder.
• Police confirm the investigation is ongoing and there is no broader threat.
