Daily Mail Royal Editor Denies Requesting Prince Harry's Flight Information

Rebecca English, the Daily Mail's royal editor, has publicly refuted allegations that she sought confidential travel details regarding Prince Harry. The statement comes in response to claims that she asked a freelance journalist to obtain the Duke of Sussex's flight information. English asserts she did not make such a request and that any information used in her reporting was sourced through other, legitimate channels. The matter touches on ongoing concerns about media practices and privacy surrounding the royal family. No further legal action or formal complaints have been reported in connection with these specific allegations at this time.

Key points:
*   Rebecca English denies asking a freelance journalist for Prince Harry's travel details.
*   She states any flight information used in reporting came from alternative sources.
*   The incident highlights continuing scrutiny of media conduct toward the royal family.
