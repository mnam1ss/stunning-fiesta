Police in Tucson, Arizona, have conducted a search operation in an area near the home of Nancy Guthrie, an 84-year-old woman who was reportedly abducted nearly two weeks ago. Authorities have not disclosed specific details about the nature of the activity or any potential findings. The investigation into Guthrie's disappearance remains active, with law enforcement continuing to follow leads in the case. Local residents have been advised that there is no immediate threat to public safety related to the police presence. Officials are urging anyone with information about the incident to come forward.
