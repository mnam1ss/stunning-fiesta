Iranian Diplomat Cautions UK on Military Stance

A senior Iranian diplomat has issued a warning to the United Kingdom regarding its potential military actions in the ongoing Middle East conflict. The ambassador stated that Iran reserves the right to protect itself should British forces engage in direct strikes alongside the United States and Israel. The comments underscore heightened regional tensions and signal Tehran's view that such participation would cross a line, potentially provoking a direct response.

The warning follows a period of increased hostilities and international concern over the conflict's expansion. Diplomatic channels remain active as global powers urge restraint to prevent a broader regional war. The UK government has not issued an immediate public response to this specific diplomatic message.

Key points:
*   An Iranian ambassador has cautioned the UK against direct military involvement in the current conflict.
*   The diplomat stated that Iran would consider such involvement a justification for defensive action.
*   The warning highlights escalating diplomatic tensions amid fears of a widening war.
