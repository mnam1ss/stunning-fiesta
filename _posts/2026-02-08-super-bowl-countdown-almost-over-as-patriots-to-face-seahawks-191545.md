**Super Bowl 60 Set for Sunday in San Francisco Bay Area**

The culmination of the American football season arrives this Sunday with Super Bowl 60. The game will be held in the San Francisco Bay Area, concluding a week of extensive pre-game events and media coverage. The New England Patriots and the Seattle Seahawks will compete for the championship.

The event is annually one of the most-watched television broadcasts in the United States. Team preparations and final practices have been conducted throughout the week leading to the Sunday night kickoff.

Key points:
*   Super Bowl 60 is scheduled for Sunday night in the San Francisco Bay Area.
*   The competing teams are the New England Patriots and the Seattle Seahawks.
*   The game concludes the National Football League season.
