**Air Travel Disrupted in Gulf Region Following Regional Strikes**

Air travel to and from the United Arab Emirates remains significantly disrupted, with a limited number of flights resuming on Monday. The ongoing regional conflict has led to the cancellation of the vast majority of services to major Middle Eastern aviation hubs. While some carriers have begun operating a reduced schedule, widespread cancellations and delays are continuing to affect passenger plans across the Gulf.

The disruption follows military strikes in the region over the weekend, which have prompted heightened security measures and airspace adjustments. Airlines are advising passengers to check directly with their carriers for the latest flight status before traveling to airports. The situation is fluid, with aviation authorities monitoring developments closely.

Key points:
-   Limited flight operations have resumed in the UAE, but extensive cancellations persist.
-   Most services to major Middle Eastern connecting hubs remain grounded.
-   Airlines are urging passengers to verify their flight status due to the volatile situation.
