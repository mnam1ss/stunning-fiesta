**Green Party Secures Victory in Greater Manchester Constituency**

In a significant electoral shift, the Green Party has won a parliamentary seat in Greater Manchester, overtaking the previously dominant Labour Party. The result, confirmed following the local count, saw Labour fall to third place after having secured over half the vote in the 2024 general election for this constituency. The victory marks a notable change in the area's political landscape, with the Greens now holding the seat. The outcome is part of broader analyses of changing voter patterns and party performance in the region. Full coverage of the election night developments, including the moment the result was declared, is available from the broadcaster.

**Key points:**
*   The Green Party won a Greater Manchester parliamentary seat, defeating the incumbent party.
*   The Labour Party, which won the seat with over 50% of the vote in 2024, finished in third place.
*   The result indicates a substantial change in voter support within the constituency.
