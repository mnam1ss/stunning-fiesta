**Retaliatory Strikes Target Sites Across Middle East, Including Dubai Hotel**

A series of strikes launched by Iran early today targeted locations across the Middle East, including a prominent hotel in Dubai. The attacks, described by Iranian state media as a retaliatory operation, resulted in visible damage to the Fairmont The Palm, a luxury resort located on an artificial island. Images from the scene showed smoke emanating from the structure. The strikes also reportedly hit several US military facilities in the region. There have been no immediate reports of casualties from the hotel incident. The action follows heightened regional tensions and appears to be a direct response to recent events. Authorities are currently assessing the extent of the damage at all affected sites.

Key points:
*   Iran conducted retaliatory strikes targeting both civilian and military sites across the Middle East.
*   A luxury hotel in Dubai sustained visible damage, with smoke and flames observed at the scene.
*   The attacks also struck several US military bases, though full casualty reports are pending.
