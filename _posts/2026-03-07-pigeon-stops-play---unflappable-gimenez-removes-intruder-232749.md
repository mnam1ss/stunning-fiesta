A pigeon briefly interrupted a Spanish football match on Saturday, causing a temporary halt in play. The incident occurred during the La Liga fixture between Atlético Madrid and Real Sociedad at the Civitas Metropolitano stadium.

With the match in progress, the bird entered the field of play, leading officials to stop the game for player safety. Atlético Madrid defender José Giménez intervened, calmly approaching the pigeon. He picked up the bird and carried it off the pitch to a secure area, allowing the match to resume shortly afterward.

The interruption did not affect the final outcome, with Atlético Madrid securing a 3-2 victory in the league contest. The unusual event was noted by broadcasters and spectators at the venue.

Key points:
- Play was temporarily stopped during a La Liga match due to a pigeon on the field.
- Atlético Madrid defender José Giménez removed the bird to safety.
- The match concluded with a 3-2 victory for Atlético Madrid over Real Sociedad.
