**Ukraine Conflict Enters Fifth Year with No Clear Path to Ceasefire**

As the conflict in Ukraine reaches the four-year mark since the escalation to full-scale war, military analysts report that a negotiated end to hostilities remains elusive. Despite significant territorial losses and a high human cost, Ukrainian forces continue to mount a determined defense across extensive front lines. Observers note that sustained international military assistance has been a critical factor in enabling Ukraine's prolonged resistance against a larger adversary.

The prospect of a formal ceasefire agreement appears distant, with both sides entrenched in their positions. Recent months have seen continued artillery duels and drone strikes, indicating no major operational pauses. The situation is characterized as a grinding war of attrition, with neither side achieving a decisive breakthrough to force an end to the fighting.

Key points:
* The war has now persisted for four years since its major escalation.
* Ukrainian forces maintain a defensive posture with ongoing international support.
* Diplomatic efforts for a lasting ceasefire have so far yielded no significant results.
