**Man Arrested in Connection with Police Investigation**

A man has been arrested and is currently in custody as part of an ongoing police operation. The arrest was made by Thames Valley Police, who are also conducting searches at properties in two counties. The locations of these searches are in Berkshire and Norfolk.

Authorities have not released the identity of the individual in custody or the specific nature of the investigation leading to the arrest. The police operation involves coordinated activity across the named regions. No further details regarding potential charges or the timeline of the investigation have been disclosed to the public at this stage.

The police force has issued a brief statement confirming these developments, stating the actions are part of their investigative procedures. The situation remains active as officers continue their work.

Key points:
- A man is in police custody following an arrest by Thames Valley Police.
- Officers are searching addresses in Berkshire and Norfolk.
- Police have not disclosed details about the investigation or any potential charges.
