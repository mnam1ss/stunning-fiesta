Reports indicate a significant escalation in regional tensions following a series of military actions. According to statements from former U.S. President Donald Trump, Iran's Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei has been killed. This claim has not been independently verified by major news outlets. Concurrently, Iranian forces have launched a substantial missile attack directed at Israel. Residents and officials in several Gulf nations, including Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, and the United Arab Emirates, also reported hearing explosions, though the origins and targets of these blasts remain unclear. The situation is developing rapidly, with no official confirmation from Iranian state media regarding the status of its leadership. International observers are closely monitoring for official responses from the involved governments.

Key points:
*   Unverified claims report the death of Iran's Supreme Leader, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei.
*   Iran has conducted retaliatory missile strikes targeting Israel.
*   Explosions were reported in multiple Gulf states, including Qatar and the UAE.
