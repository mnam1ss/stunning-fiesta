Three major films are set to compete for the top prize at a major Hollywood awards ceremony this Sunday. The contenders include the drama "Sinners," the action film "One Battle After Another," and the historical story "Hamnet." Industry analysts note that the outcome is considered highly unpredictable, with each film representing a distinct genre and having garnered significant critical acclaim during the previous awards season. The ceremony will conclude a months-long campaign period where studios have promoted their nominated works. The final result is determined by a vote from the academy's membership body.

Key points:
• The final award ceremony takes place this Sunday.
• The primary contenders are "Sinners," "One Battle After Another," and "Hamnet."
• The winner is selected by an academy voting body.
