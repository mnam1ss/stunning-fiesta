**K-pop Group Makes Grammy History as Ceremony Begins**

The 66th Annual Grammy Awards commenced in Los Angeles with a historic win for the South Korean ensemble K-pop Demon Hunters. The group secured the first award of the night, marking a significant milestone in the ceremony's recognition of international artists.

Early awards also saw several British artists honored. Veteran rock band The Cure, musician Yungblud, and performer FKA Twigs were among the first recipients from the United Kingdom during the initial broadcast segment.

The evening's events are ongoing at the Crypto.com Arena, with numerous categories still to be announced. The early wins have set a tone of diverse international representation for the prestigious music awards show.

Key points:
*   K-pop Demon Hunters won an early award, making Grammy history.
*   British artists The Cure, Yungblud, and FKA twigs were also among the first winners.
*   The ceremony continues in Los Angeles with more awards to be presented.
