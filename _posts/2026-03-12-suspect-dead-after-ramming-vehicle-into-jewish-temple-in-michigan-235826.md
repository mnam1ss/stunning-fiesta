**Vehicle Rams Michigan Synagogue; Suspect Deceased**

A suspect is dead after driving a vehicle into a Jewish temple in Michigan, authorities report. The incident occurred when the individual crashed into the synagogue, causing the vehicle to catch fire. Emergency services responded to the scene. Police officials stated that the suspect was pronounced dead at the location. The investigation is ongoing, and law enforcement has not yet publicly released the identity of the individual involved. Officials have also not determined a specific motive for the attack. There were no immediate reports of other injuries. The synagogue sustained damage from both the impact and the subsequent fire. Federal authorities are assisting local police with the inquiry.

Key points:
- A suspect died after crashing a vehicle into a Michigan synagogue, igniting a fire.
- Police have not released the suspect's name or identified a motive.
- The investigation involves local and federal authorities; no other injuries were reported.
