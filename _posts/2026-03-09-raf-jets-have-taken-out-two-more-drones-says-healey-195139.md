Defence Secretary John Healey has informed Parliament that Royal Air Force fighter jets have intercepted and destroyed two additional unmanned aerial vehicles over the Middle East. The engagement is part of ongoing operations in the region.

In a separate development, Mr. Healey confirmed the imminent deployment of the Royal Navy's Type 45 destroyer, HMS Dragon. The warship is scheduled to depart for the Mediterranean Sea within the coming days to undertake unspecified duties.

The announcements were made during a routine ministerial update to Members of Parliament, detailing recent military activities and future deployments.

Key points:
• RAF aircraft successfully engaged two more drones in the Middle East.
• The destroyer HMS Dragon will soon deploy to the Mediterranean.
• The Defence Secretary provided these updates in a statement to MPs.
