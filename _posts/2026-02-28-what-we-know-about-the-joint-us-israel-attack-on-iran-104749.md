**Reports Detail Regional Strikes Following US-Israel Military Action**

Military activity escalated across the Middle East early Friday following a previously reported joint operation by the United States and Israel targeting sites within Iran. In response, Iranian forces launched a significant number of missiles toward Israeli territory.

Concurrently, residents and officials in several Gulf nations, including Qatar, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates, reported hearing loud explosions. The exact nature and cause of these blasts have not been officially confirmed by all affected states. The developments mark a serious expansion of regional hostilities that began with an Iranian attack on Israel last weekend.

Key points:
*   Iran fired missiles at Israel in retaliation for a US-Israel strike on Iranian targets.
*   Explosions were reported in multiple Gulf states, though their origins are unclear.
*   The exchange represents a major escalation of cross-border attacks in the region.
