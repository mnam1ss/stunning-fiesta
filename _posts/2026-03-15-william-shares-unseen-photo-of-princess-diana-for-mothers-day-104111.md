Prince William has released a previously unpublished photograph of his mother, the late Diana, Princess of Wales, to mark Mother's Day in the United Kingdom. The image, shared on the Prince and Princess of Wales's official social media accounts, shows a young Diana smiling while seated in a garden. In an accompanying message, the Prince of Wales reflected on her memory, stating he thinks of her both on this day and regularly throughout the year. The personal tribute has drawn significant public attention, with many noting the timing of the release on a day dedicated to celebrating mothers. The photograph offers a rare, informal glimpse into the prince's private family archives.

Key points:
*   Prince William shared a new photograph of Princess Diana for UK Mother's Day.
*   He included a personal message about remembering her.
*   The release has sparked widespread public and media interest.
