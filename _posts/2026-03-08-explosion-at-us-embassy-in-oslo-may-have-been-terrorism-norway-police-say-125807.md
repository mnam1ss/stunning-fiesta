**Explosion at U.S. Embassy in Oslo Under Investigation as Potential Terror Act**

Norwegian police are investigating an explosion that occurred at the U.S. embassy in Oslo on Wednesday. Authorities have stated the blast caused minor damage to the embassy building but no injuries have been reported.

In a briefing, police officials confirmed they are treating the incident as a possible act of terrorism and are exploring all potential motives. The area around the embassy was cordoned off for a security sweep following the detonation. An investigation is ongoing to determine the exact nature of the explosive device and identify those responsible.

The U.S. State Department has acknowledged the incident and is cooperating with local authorities. Embassy operations were temporarily affected but are expected to resume normally.

Key points:
*   Norwegian police are investigating the explosion as a possible terrorist act.
