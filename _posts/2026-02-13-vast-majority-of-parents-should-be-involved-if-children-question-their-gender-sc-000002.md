New guidance for schools in England advises that parents should generally be informed if a child expresses uncertainty about their gender identity. The Department for Education issued the long-awaited framework to help educators navigate this sensitive topic, aiming to provide consistent practices across schools.

The guidance states that parents should be involved in such situations unless there is a significant risk of harm to the child.
