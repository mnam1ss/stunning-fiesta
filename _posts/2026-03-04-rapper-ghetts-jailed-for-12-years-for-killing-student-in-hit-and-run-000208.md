Grime artist Ghetts has been sentenced to 12 years in prison for a fatal hit-and-run incident. The musician, whose real name is Justin Clarke, was driving a BMW when he struck 20-year-old Nepali student Yubin Tamang in Ilford, east London. Clarke did not stop at the scene following the collision.

The 39-year-old rapper was convicted of causing death by dangerous driving and perverting the course of justice. During the trial, the court heard that Clarke had been driving at excessive speed in a residential area prior to the crash. He later made attempts to conceal his involvement in the incident.
