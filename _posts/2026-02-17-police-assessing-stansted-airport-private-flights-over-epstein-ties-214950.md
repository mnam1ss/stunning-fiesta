Essex Police have confirmed they are examining information regarding private aircraft activity at Stansted Airport. The assessment is connected to wider inquiries into the activities of the late financier Jeffrey Epstein. Authorities are reviewing flight data and records for private jets that used the airport.

The force stated the review is a standard procedure when potentially relevant information is received as part of an ongoing investigation. No specific flights or individuals have been publicly identified in connection with this assessment. Stansted Airport, a major hub for private aviation in the UK, has stated it will cooperate fully with the police.

Key points:
• Essex Police are assessing information on private flights at Stansted Airport.
• The review is linked to investigations concerning Jeffrey Epstein.
• No specific details about flights or individuals have been released.
