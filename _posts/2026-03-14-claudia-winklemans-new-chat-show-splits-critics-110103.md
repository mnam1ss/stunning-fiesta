Claudia Winkleman's new television talk show has received a mixed response from reviewers following its first broadcast. The premiere episode featured interviews with actors Jeff Goldblum and Vanessa Williams, alongside comedian Jennifer Saunders and presenter Tom Allen. While some critics praised the program's relaxed atmosphere and Winkleman's conversational style, others found the format lacking in energy and substantive discussion. The show represents a significant new presenting role for Winkleman, who is widely known for her work on other major entertainment programs. The critical divide suggests the series may polarize audiences as it continues its run.

Key points:
* The debut of Claudia Winkleman's talk show has drawn both positive and negative reviews from television critics.
* The inaugural episode included guests Jeff Goldblum, Vanessa Williams, Jennifer Saunders, and Tom Allen.
* Critical opinions varied, with some applauding the host's approach and others critiquing the pace and depth of conversation.
