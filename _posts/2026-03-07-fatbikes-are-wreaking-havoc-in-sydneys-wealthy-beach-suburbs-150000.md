**Fatbike Disruptions Reported in Sydney Coastal Areas**

Residents in several affluent beachside suburbs of Sydney have reported ongoing disturbances involving teenagers riding oversized "fatbike" bicycles. The incidents, which have drawn local complaints, include riders trespassing on private golf courses and performing stunts such as wheelies on pedestrian pathways of the Sydney Harbour Bridge.

Local authorities have noted an increase in related complaints over recent months. The bicycles, characterized by their wide tires, are reportedly being used in a manner that conflicts with existing bylaws governing public spaces and private property. Community discussions are focused on balancing recreational cycling with the maintenance of public order and safety.

Key points:
- Teenagers on fatbikes are causing disturbances in Sydney's coastal suburbs.
- Reported incidents include trespassing on golf courses and stunt riding on the Harbour Bridge.
- Local officials are addressing community complaints regarding public safety and property use.
