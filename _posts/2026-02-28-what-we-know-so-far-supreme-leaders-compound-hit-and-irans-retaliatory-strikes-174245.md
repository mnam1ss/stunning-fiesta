**Supreme Leader's Residence Reportedly Struck Amid Regional Escalation**

Reports indicate a compound associated with Iran's Supreme Leader was targeted overnight. In response, Iranian forces launched a significant missile attack toward Israel. Concurrently, residents in several Gulf nations, including Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, and the United Arab Emirates, reported hearing explosions, though the origins and any potential damage remain unclear. The incidents mark a sharp escalation in regional tensions following recent hostilities. Authorities in the affected countries have not yet issued detailed statements regarding the events. The situation remains fluid as international observers monitor for further developments and official confirmations.

Key points:
*   A facility linked to Iran's Supreme Leader was reportedly hit, prompting Iranian missile launches toward Israel.
*   Explosions were reported in multiple Gulf states, including Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, and the UAE.
*   The events signify a major increase in regional hostilities, with details still emerging.
