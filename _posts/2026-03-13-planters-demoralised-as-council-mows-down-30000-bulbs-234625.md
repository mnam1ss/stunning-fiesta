**Council Launches Inquiry After Newly Planted Bulbs Mown Down**

The city council has initiated an internal investigation following an incident where municipal maintenance staff mowed over an estimated 30,000 recently planted flower bulbs. The bulbs, which had been in the ground for only a few days, were destroyed during routine grass-cutting operations.

Local volunteers involved in the planting initiative expressed significant disappointment, stating the effort was intended to enhance community green spaces. Council representatives have acknowledged the error and confirmed the review aims to determine the circumstances that led to the mishap and to prevent a recurrence. No specific timeline for the investigation's conclusion has been provided.

Key points:
- An internal council investigation is underway after workers accidentally destroyed thousands of newly planted flower bulbs.
- Community planters involved in the project have reported feeling demoralized by the loss.
- The council states the review will seek to clarify procedures and avoid similar incidents in the future.
