**Student Fatally Stabbed Near University Campus; Teenager Arrested**

An 18-year-old man has been taken into custody following a fatal stabbing incident near the campus of De Montfort University in Leicester. The victim, a male student, was pronounced dead at the scene. The arrest was made on suspicion of murder, and the suspect remains in police custody for questioning.

Local authorities have cordoned off the area as part of their ongoing investigation. Police have stated they are not currently seeking any other individuals in connection with the death but are appealing to the public for any relevant information. University officials have confirmed they are providing support to the student community and are cooperating fully with the police investigation.

Key points:
*   An 18-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder.
*   The incident occurred near De Montfort University's campus, resulting in the death of a student.
*   Police are continuing their investigation and have appealed for information from the public.
