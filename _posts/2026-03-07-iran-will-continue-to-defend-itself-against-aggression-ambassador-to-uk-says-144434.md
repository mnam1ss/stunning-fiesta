Iran's ambassador to the United Kingdom has stated the country will maintain its right to self-defense in response to any hostile actions. In a televised interview, Seyed Ali Mousavi indicated that future Iranian actions would be contingent on the conduct of the United States and Israel. The remarks follow a period of heightened regional tensions and recent military exchanges.

The ambassador's comments underscore the ongoing diplomatic and security challenges in the Middle East. He framed Iran's potential responses as reactive measures, directly linking them to the perceived actions of other nations. This statement contributes to the public discourse surrounding the volatile situation.

Key points:
* Iran's ambassador says the country reserves the right to defend itself against aggression.
* He stated that Iran's future actions will depend on what the US and Israel do.
* The comments were made during a broadcast interview amid ongoing regional tensions.
