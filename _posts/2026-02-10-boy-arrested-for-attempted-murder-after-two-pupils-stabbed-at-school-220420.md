**Boy Detained Following School Stabbing Incident**

A teenage boy has been arrested on suspicion of attempted murder after two pupils were stabbed at a school in northeast England. The incident occurred on Monday morning, resulting in both victims, also teenage boys, sustaining serious injuries. Emergency services responded to the scene, and the injured students were transported to a hospital for treatment.

Authorities have confirmed that the investigation is being led by counter-terrorism police, though they have emphasized that the motive remains unclear and they are keeping an open mind. The suspect, who cannot be named due to his age, is currently in custody. The school was secured following the attack, and police have stated there is no ongoing threat to the wider community.
