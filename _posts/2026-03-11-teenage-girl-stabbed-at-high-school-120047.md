**Student Injured in Stabbing Incident at High School**

A teenage girl has been hospitalized following a stabbing at a high school earlier today. The incident prompted an immediate emergency response, with police and medical personnel arriving at the scene. School officials implemented security protocols, directing students and staff to remain inside classrooms and other buildings as a precautionary measure while authorities secured the area.

The injured student was transported to a local hospital for treatment. The condition of the victim has not been officially released. Police have confirmed they are investigating the circumstances surrounding the attack and that a suspect has been taken into custody. The school day was disrupted, with parents being notified of the situation. An investigation by law enforcement is ongoing.

*   A teenage girl was stabbed at a high school and taken to a hospital.
