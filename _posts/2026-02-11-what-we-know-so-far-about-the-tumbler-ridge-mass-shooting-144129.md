**Shooting Incidents in Western Canada Leave Multiple Casualties**

A series of shootings in the rural community of Tumbler Ridge, British Columbia, has resulted in numerous fatalities and injuries. The incidents occurred at a school and a private residence. According to official reports, nine people have died and 25 others were wounded in the violence.

Emergency services responded to the scenes, and the area remains under a significant police presence. Authorities have not released details about a potential suspect or a motive for the attacks. An investigation involving multiple agencies is ongoing.

Community leaders have expressed shock and are coordinating support services for residents. The local school district has confirmed the involvement of one of its buildings and is providing counseling resources.

Key points:
*   Nine fatalities and 25 injuries have been reported.
*   The incidents occurred at a school and a home in Tumbler Ridge, B.C.
*   A police investigation is active; no motive or suspect details have been released.
