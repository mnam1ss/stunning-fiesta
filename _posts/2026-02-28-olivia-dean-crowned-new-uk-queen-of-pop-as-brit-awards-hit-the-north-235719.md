Olivia Dean was the major winner at the 2024 Brit Awards, which were held outside London for the first time in the ceremony's history. The event took place in Manchester on Saturday night. The singer-songwriter secured four awards, the most of any artist, taking home the trophies for Best Artist and Mastercard Album of the Year for her debut, "Messy." Her other wins included Best Pop Act and Best New Artist.

The relocation to Manchester's Co-op Live arena was a significant shift for the UK's primary music awards show, marking a deliberate effort to decentralize from the capital. The ceremony featured performances from artists including Raye, who also won the Song of the Year award, and Dua Lipa.

**Key points:**
*   Olivia Dean won four Brit Awards, including Best Artist and Album of the Year.
*   The 2024 ceremony was held in Manchester, the first time it has taken place outside London.
*   Raye received the Song of the Year award for "Escapism."
