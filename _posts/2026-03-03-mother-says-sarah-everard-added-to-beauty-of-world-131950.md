**Mother Honors Daughter's Memory on Fifth Anniversary of Murder**

On the fifth anniversary of her daughter's death, Susan Everard has publicly reflected on the life of Sarah Everard. In a statement, she described her daughter as a person who contributed to the beauty of the world. Sarah Everard was murdered in March 2021 by a serving Metropolitan Police officer, an event that prompted widespread public grief and national debates concerning violence against women and police accountability.

The anniversary serves as a somber reminder of the case, which led to significant protests and calls for systemic change regarding women's safety. No official public events were reported to mark the date, with the family's personal tribute being the primary public acknowledgment.

Key points:
*   Susan Everard has paid tribute to her daughter Sarah five years after her murder.
*   Sarah Everard's death in 2021 sparked national outcry and discussions on women's safety.
*   The convicted murderer was a serving London police officer at the time of the crime.
