**Royal Family Responds to Duke of York's Arrest**

Buckingham Palace has issued a statement following the arrest of the Duke of York, Prince Andrew. The statement, released on behalf of King Charles III, confirms that the Royal Family is cooperating fully with the ongoing legal process. It emphasizes that the relevant authorities have the family's complete support as the matter proceeds.

The arrest is connected to a long-running investigation, the details of which are being handled by official channels. The Palace has declined to comment further on the specifics of the case, citing the active nature of the proceedings. This development marks a significant legal step in a situation that has drawn considerable public attention over recent years.

Key points:
*   The Royal Family has pledged its full cooperation with authorities.
*   King Charles III has authorized a statement supporting the legal process.
*   The arrest relates to an existing investigation, with no further details provided by the Palace.
