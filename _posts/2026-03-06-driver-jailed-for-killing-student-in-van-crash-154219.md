A court has sentenced a man to prison following a fatal collision that resulted in the death of a university student. Christopher Jackson was operating a van when it struck and killed 20-year-old Aalia Mahomed. The incident occurred earlier this year, leading to a police investigation and subsequent legal proceedings. During the trial, evidence was presented detailing the circumstances of the crash. The judge presiding over the case determined that Jackson's actions warranted a custodial sentence. The ruling concludes a legal process that began after the young woman was pronounced dead at the scene. No further details regarding the length of the sentence or the specific charges were immediately released in the initial summary.

Key points:
• A man has been imprisoned for causing a fatal van collision.
• The victim was identified as Aalia Mahomed, a 20-year-old student.
• The driver, Christopher Jackson, was sentenced following a court trial.
