UK Defence Secretary John Healey has stated that recent military strikes by Iran in the Middle East have endangered British troops and civilians in the region. He reported that personnel stationed at a UK base in Bahrain were located very close to the impact zone of one such strike.

The comments highlight the immediate risks to allied forces amid ongoing regional tensions. The UK maintains several military installations across the Middle East, which are involved in various security and training operations.

**Key points:**
*   The UK Defence Secretary reported that British forces in Bahrain were in close proximity to a recent Iranian strike.
*   The incident underscores the heightened risk to military and civilian personnel stationed in the region.
*   The statement comes amid a period of significant instability and conflict in the Middle East.
