**Royal Family Issues Statement Following Duke's Arrest**

Buckingham Palace has released a statement on behalf of King Charles III following the arrest of his brother, the Duke of York, on undisclosed charges. The statement confirms that the Royal Family is aware of the situation and is cooperating with the ongoing legal process. It further expresses the family's intention to support the work of the authorities involved.

The brief palace communication did not provide details on the nature of the allegations or the Duke's current status. Legal representatives for the Duke have not yet issued a public comment. The development marks a significant legal challenge for a senior royal and is likely to prompt further public and media scrutiny of the monarchy's activities.

Key points:
*   King Charles III has stated the Royal Family is cooperating with authorities.
*   The specific charges against the Duke of York have not been publicly detailed.
*   The arrest presents a notable legal and reputational event for the monarchy.
