**Trump Speaks with Starmer as Allies React to Iran Strikes**

President Donald Trump has spoken with UK Labour leader Sir Keir Starmer, following military strikes against Iran conducted by Israel and the United States. The conversation between the US president and the British opposition leader comes amid differing international responses to the recent military action. According to official statements from the United Kingdom, the British government was not a participant in the strikes and has not given its formal approval for the operations. The call highlights the ongoing diplomatic engagements between US and European leaders, even as their public positions on the situation appear to diverge. Further details regarding the substance of the discussion have not been disclosed by either party.

Key points:
- US President Donald Trump and UK Labour leader Keir Starmer held a phone call.
- The UK government stated it was not involved in and did not endorse the recent strikes on Iran.
- The call occurs amidst visible differences in US and European stances on the military action.
