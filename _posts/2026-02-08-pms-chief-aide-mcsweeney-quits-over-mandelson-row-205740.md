**Senior Political Adviser Resigns Following Diplomatic Appointment Controversy**

A senior adviser to the Prime Minister has resigned from his position. The departure follows internal discussions regarding the proposed diplomatic appointment of a prominent Labour peer.

The adviser stated he accepts complete accountability for his role in recommending the peer for a key ambassadorial role in the United States. The recommendation reportedly caused significant political friction within the government.

The Prime Minister's office has acknowledged the resignation. No immediate successor has been named, and the future of the ambassadorial appointment remains unclear following this development.

Key points:
*   The Prime Minister's chief political adviser has resigned.
*   The resignation is linked to advice given concerning a proposed ambassadorial posting.
*   The adviser stated he bears full responsibility for the recommendation.
